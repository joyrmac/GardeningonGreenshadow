## Sunday — House Prep & Rest Day

**This is your reset day. Treat it as sacred.**

- **7:30-9:00** — Slow morning, coffee, minimal rush
- **9:00-10:30** — **HOUSE PREP BLOCK** (with family helping)
  - Laundry (sort, start)
  - Tidy living spaces
  - Prep lunches for the week (or plan them)
  - Stock fridge for the week (snack prep)
  - Murphy & Birdie help (age-appropriate tasks)
  - Chad does whatever heavy lifting / organization needed
- **10:30-12:00** — **OUTSIDE TIME** (park, walk, yard work, gardening — whatever resets you)
- **12:00-1:00** — Lunch, family time
- **1:00-3:00** — **REST TIME** (this is for YOU)
  - You: read, yoga, journaling, nap, spiritual practice, whatever fills you up
  - Kids: quiet time (reading, puzzles, low-key activities)
- **3:00-4:30** — Light family activity or prep for week

## Adjustments & Notes

- **Transitions for kids need warnings:** "In 10 minutes we're leaving" → "5 minutes" → "2 minutes"
