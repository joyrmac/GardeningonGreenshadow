## Important Clarifications

### 🌙 Astrology vs. Practical

**Astrology files** (`/astrology/`) are for:
- Understanding WHY your kids are who they are
- Spiritual context and self-reflection
- Finding meaning in patterns
- NOT for making parenting decisions alone

**Parenting files** (`/parenting/`) are for:
- WHAT to do when things are hard
- Specific strategies and language
- How to respond in moments
- Uses astrology as context, focuses on action

**They work together.** Astrology says "Murphy has an Aries Moon, which means she processes emotions fast." Parenting says "So redirect her to physical activity when she's ramped up." Tips bridges both.

### 📋 Format Notes

**Practical guides** are formatted for quick reference:
- Situation → What's happening → What to do
- Quick reference cards
- Specific language/scripts to use

**Astrology readings** are formatted for deeper understanding:
- Full birth chart interpretation
- Transits and timing
- Spiritual themes
