# Notes from Previous Owner (Kelli)

> Tips and observations from Kelli, who lived in the house before us. Useful for understanding what already grows on the property and what works where.

---

## Frost & Planting Timing

- **Do not plant anything until after April 15.** It continues to freeze and you will lose plants. She learned this the hard way multiple times.

## What's Already Growing on the Property

- Large **red lilies** in the back
- Lots of **daisies** and **black-eyed Susans**
- **Centipede or zoysia** grass (turf grass, goes dormant in winter, comes back in May)

## What Worked Well

- **Clematis** — grew on tomato planter for height
- **Hibiscus** — also used for height
- **Geraniums** — thrived in full sun and heat
- **Snowflake** — great trailing/growing down
- **Petunias** — also good flowing down
- She did different plants each time (rotated what she grew)

## Specific Spots

- **Under trees by garden:** Basil and cilantro do well in pots here
- **Kitchen window brick box:** Peppers thrive in this spot
