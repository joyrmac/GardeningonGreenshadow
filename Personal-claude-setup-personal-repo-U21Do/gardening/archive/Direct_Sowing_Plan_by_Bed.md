# 🌱 DIRECT SOWING PLAN BY GARDEN BED  
  
  
**Absolutely, babe! Let’s break it all down by garden bed so you know exactly what to plant where and when.** 🌱✨ **Everything will be direct sown**—no babying inside, just straight into the earth where it belongs! 🌿🔥  
  
**🌿 Spiral Herb Garden (With Frog Pond)**  
📅 **Sow Late March - Early April** (Soil 50°F+) ✔️ **Top (Dry, Sunny, Windy)**  
* 🌿 Rosemary  
* 🌿 Thyme  
* 🌿 Oregano  
✔️ **Southwest (Full Sun)**  
* 🌿 Sage  
* 🌿 Lavender  
* 🌿 Basil  
✔️ **East (Morning Sun, Partial Shade)**  
* 🌿 Parsley  
* 🌿 Chives  
✔️ **North (Shady, Moist)**  
* 🌿 Cilantro  
* 🌿 Dill  
* 🌿 Lemon Balm  
✔️ **Frog Pond Area (Wet-Tolerant Plants)**  
* 🌿 Water Iris  
* 🌿 Pickerel Weed  
* 🌿 Creeping Jenny  
💡 **Planting Tip**: Scatter herb seeds in their designated zones, and mulch lightly to hold in moisture!  
  
**🦋 Native Pollinator Garden**  
📅 **Sow Early March - April** (Cold stratify perennials if planting late) ✔️ **Tall Back Layer** (For Height & Structure)  
* 🌸 **Joe Pye Weed**  
* 🌸 **Goldenrod**  
* 🌸 **Wild Senna**  
✔️ **Mid-Layer (Pollinator Nectar Powerhouses)**  
* 🌸 **Bee Balm (Wild Bergamot)**  
* 🌸 **Black-Eyed Susan**  
* 🌸 **Balloon Milkweed**  
✔️ **Front Layer (Ground Cover & Accent Blooms)**  
* 🌸 **Tweedia (Heavenly Blue)**  
* 🌸 **Yarrow (Summer Berries)**  
* 🌸 **Milkweed (Gay Butterflies)**  
💡 **Planting Tip**: Scatter **Milkweed, Goldenrod, and Joe Pye Weed** in late winter for natural stratification.  
  
**🏡 East-Facing 12’x2’ Bed (Bay Leaf Tree Center)**  
📅 **Sow April - May** ✔️ **Drought-Tolerant Herbs & Perennials**  
* 🌿 Lavender  
* 🌿 Thyme  
* 🌿 Yarrow  
✔️ **Companion for Bay Tree**  
* 🌸 Nasturtiums  
* 🌸 Calendula  
* 🌿 Oregano  
💡 **Planting Tip**: Since it’s **narrow**, use **trellises for Climbing Jasmine or Morning Glories** to grow up!  
  
**🌞 10x15 ft Sunny Bed (Screen Room Front)**  
📅 **Sow Mid-March - May** ✔️ **East Side (Full Sun, Nectar-Rich Blooms)**  
* 🌻 Sunflowers (Autumn Beauty)  
* 🌸 Coneflowers (Blueberry Cheesecake, Pink Double Delight, Marmalade, Parrot, Green Wizard)  
* 🌿 Milkweed  
✔️ **West Side (Partial Shade)**  
* 🌸 Coreopsis  
* 🌸 Nasturtiums  
✔️ **North Side (Less Direct Sun)**  
* 🌿 Black Cohosh  
* 🌸 Balloon Milkweed  
✔️ **South Side (Dappled Light, Lower Plants)**  
* 🌸 Yarrow  
* 🌸 Lobelia  
💡 **Planting Tip**: **Coneflowers & Sunflowers** will **self-seed for next year** if left to dry!  
  
**🌑 6x4 ft Shaded Bed (Next to Screen Room)**  
📅 **Sow Late March - April** ✔️ **East Side (Light Sun, Partial Shade Tolerant)**  
* 🌿 Coral Bells  
* 🌸 Columbine  
✔️ **North Side (Deep Shade, Against the Screen Room)**  
* 🌿 Hostas  
* 🌸 Lily-of-the-Valley  
* 🌿 Christmas Fern  
✔️ **West Side (Shade-Tolerant Accent Plants)**  
* 🌿 Elephant Ears  
* 🌸 Bleeding Heart  
✔️ **South Side (Partial Shade, Medium-Height Flowers)**  
* 🌸 Foxglove  
* 🌸 Astilbe  
💡 **Planting Tip**: Use a **light layer of mulch** to retain moisture.  
  
**🌾 Backyard Prairie Garden**  
📅 **Sow Late February - March** (Scatter Seeds Over Existing Soil) ✔️ **Native Perennials for Habitat**  
* 🌿 Joe Pye Weed  
* 🌿 Wild Bergamot  
* 🌿 Black-Eyed Susans  
✔️ **Pollinator Wildflowers**  
* 🌸 Coneflowers  
* 🌸 Cosmos (Sea Shells, Apricotta, Mandarin Orange)  
* 🌸 Zinnias (Cupcake Pink, Giant Double Enchantress)  
💡 **Planting Tip**: Once established, this area **requires very little maintenance**—just enjoy the show!  
  
**🍂 Pumpkin Patch**  
📅 **Sow Late April - Early May** ✔️ **Pumpkins & Squash (Large, Sprawling Vines)**  
* 🎃 Jack O’Lantern  
* 🎃 Small Sugar  
* 🎃 Spaghetti Squash  
✔️ **Companion Plants**  
* 🌸 Nasturtiums (Pest Control)  
* 🌿 Radishes (Soil Aeration)  
💡 **Planting Tip**: **Plant in mounds** for warmth & good drainage.  
  
**🥕 Vegetable Garden**  
📅 **Sow March - May** ✔️ **Early Spring Crops (March - April)**  
* 🥕 Carrots (All varieties)  
* 🥦 Broccoli  
* 🥬 Kale, Lettuce, Spinach  
✔️ **Warm-Season Crops (April - May)**  
* 🌽 Corn (All varieties)  
* 🍉 Watermelon (Bush Sugar Baby, Garden Leader Monster)  
* 🍅 Tomatoes (Cherokee Purple, Yellow Pear)  
* 🌶️ Peppers (Jalapeño, Shishito, Poblano)  
💡 **Planting Tip**: Use **companion planting**—tomatoes & basil love each other!  
  
**🌸 Cut Flower Garden**  
📅 **Sow April - May** ✔️ **Fast Bloomers**  
* 🌸 Zinnias (California Giant, Tequila Lime)  
* 🌸 Cosmos (Candy Stripe, Sensation Mix)  
✔️ **Classic Cut Flowers**  
* 🌸 Dahlias (Pompon Mix, Pacific View)  
* 🌸 Snapdragons  
💡 **Planting Tip**: Cut often to **encourage reblooming!**  
  
**🍉 Watermelon & Cantaloupe Patch**  
📅 **Sow Late April - May** ✔️ **Melons & Companion Plants**  
* 🍉 Bush Sugar Baby  
* 🍉 Garden Leader Monster  
* 🍈 Sierra Gold Cantaloupe  
✔️ **Companions**  
* 🌸 Nasturtiums (Pest Control)  
* 🌿 Radishes (Soil Aeration)  
💡 **Planting Tip**: Use **hills for better drainage & heat retention.**  
  
**🌞 Full Sun Landscaping (West-Facing Front Yard)**  
📅 **Sow April - May** ✔️ **Heat-Tolerant, Drought-Resistant Plants**  
* 🌸 Coneflowers  
* 🌸 Black-Eyed Susans  
* 🌿 Creeping Thyme  
💡 **Planting Tip**: **Mulch heavily** to retain moisture!  
  
**📫 Mailbox & Extra Landscaping Areas**  
📅 **Sow April - May** ✔️ **Bright, Low-Maintenance Flowers**  
* 🌸 Zinnias  
* 🌸 Nasturtium  
* 🌸 Poppies  
💡 **Planting Tip**: **Low-water plants thrive in this spot!**  
  
**🔥 YOU ARE READY TO SOW IT ALL!** Scatter, rake, water, and let Mother Nature do her thing! 🌱✨ **Your yard is about to be a whole ecosystem, babe!** 💚💃🔥
