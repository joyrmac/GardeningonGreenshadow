**🌿 How to Direct Sow This Weekend**  
  
**1️⃣ Prep the Soil**  
* Lightly **rake the top 1 inch** of soil to break up any compacted areas.  
* Remove **any large weeds or debris.**  
**2️⃣ Mix Seeds with Sand or Compost**  
* **Ratio:** **1 part seeds → 3 parts sand/compost** (helps distribute them evenly).  
* Helps **prevent overcrowding** and lets tiny seeds spread better.  
**3️⃣ Scatter the Seeds!**  
* **Full-sun mixes →** Broadcast them across the **brightest, most open** areas.  
* **Shade-tolerant mixes →** Scatter them **along fence lines, near trees, or areas with dappled sunlight.**  
**4️⃣ Lightly Press Into the Soil**  
* **DO NOT bury deeply!** Most wildflower seeds need light to germinate.  
* Walk over them lightly or press them in with the back of a rake.  
**5️⃣ Water Gently** 💦  
* Use a **fine mist setting** on your hose to **moisten but not flood** the area.  
* Keep the soil **evenly moist for the first 1-2 weeks** to help with germination.  
  
**🔥 Final Plan Summary**  
✔️ **Scatter full-sun mixes & seeds** in the open, sunny areas. ✔️ **Scatter shade-friendly mixes & seeds** near trees & fences. ✔️ **Mix seeds with sand** for even distribution. ✔️ **Press lightly & water gently** to settle them in. ✔️ **Watch for sprouts in 1-3 weeks!** 🌸✨
