# The McNamara Grimoire
### A Personal Book of Shadows — Table of Contents & Starter Framework

---

## What Is a Grimoire?

A grimoire (also called a Book of Shadows) is your personal spiritual reference book — a collection of everything you learn, practice, and discover on your spiritual path. It's part recipe book, part journal, part encyclopedia, part spell book. There is no "right" way to organize one. This starter framework gives you sections to fill in over time.

Think of it as your spiritual operating system. Everything you learn gets documented here so you don't have to remember it all — you just have to know where to look.

---

## How to Use This Grimoire

**This is a living document.** It grows as you grow. Some sections will be full immediately (herbs you already know, rituals you already practice). Others will fill in over months and years as you learn.

**You can keep this digitally or transfer it to a physical book.** Many practitioners prefer a handwritten book — there's something powerful about physically writing your knowledge. Others prefer digital for searchability and ease of updating. Both are valid.

**This framework is organized by topic.** Jump to whatever section is relevant. You don't need to fill it in order.

## XI. Protection & Energy Work

### Quick Grounding Techniques

**5-4-3-2-1 Sensory Grounding:**
- 5 things you can see
- 4 things you can touch
- 3 things you can hear
- 2 things you can smell
- 1 thing you can taste

**Tree Roots Visualization:**
- Stand or sit with feet flat on the floor
- Imagine roots growing from the soles of your feet, deep into the earth
- Feel the earth's stability rising up through your roots into your body
- Breathe deeply for 5 breaths

**Physical Grounding:**
- Press bare feet into grass, soil, or sand
- Hold a heavy stone in your hand
- Run cold water over your wrists
- Eat something earthy (root vegetables, chocolate, bread)

### Shielding Techniques (for Empaths)

**The Bubble Shield:**
- Close your eyes and take 3 deep breaths
- Visualize a bubble of white or golden light surrounding your entire body
- See it extending about 2 feet around you in all directions
- Set the intention: "This shield allows love in and keeps others' energy out"
- Refresh as needed throughout the day

**The Mirror Shield:**
- Visualize the outside of your bubble as mirrored — reflective
- Other people's energy bounces off and returns to them
- Useful for environments with heavy or negative energy

**The Filter Shield:**
- Visualize your shield as semi-permeable — like a coffee filter
- Love, kindness, and positive energy flow through
- Negativity, anxiety, and others' emotional weight stay out

**Joy — you need these.** Your Pisces Moon makes you one of the most absorbent empaths in any room. Shielding before entering crowded spaces, client meetings, or emotionally charged situations is self-care, not self-indulgence.

## XIII. Recipes & Formulas

### Moon Water

**What it is:** Water charged under moonlight. Used in rituals, cleansing, plant care, and more.

**How to make it:**
1. Fill a clear glass jar with filtered water
2. Set it outside or on a windowsill under the moon (Full Moon is most potent)
3. Optional: add a crystal on top of the lid (clear quartz, moonstone) — NOT inside the water unless you've confirmed it's water-safe
4. Set an intention for the water
5. Collect before sunrise
6. Store in the fridge for up to 2 weeks

**Uses:**
- Add to bath water for ritual baths
- Use to water plants
- Add to room sprays
- Drink (if made with filtered/safe water and no crystals were submerged)
- Use to cleanse crystals (water-safe ones only)
- Anoint candles or ritual tools

### Basic Room Spray

- 4 oz spray bottle
- 3 oz distilled water or moon water
- 1 oz witch hazel (acts as preservative and emulsifier)
- 10-15 drops essential oil (choose based on intention)

**Blend Ideas:**
- **Peace:** Lavender + chamomile + ylang ylang
- **Clearing:** Rosemary + lemon + eucalyptus
- **Protection:** Cedarwood + frankincense + black pepper
- **Love:** Rose + vanilla + jasmine
- **Focus:** Peppermint + rosemary + lemon

### Ritual Bath Recipe Template

1. Set the mood: candles, music, dim lights
2. Draw warm (not hot) water
3. Add: ½ cup Epsom salt + ½ cup baking soda
4. Add: Herbs (in a muslin bag to avoid clogging the drain) or 5-10 drops essential oil
5. Optional: Flower petals floating on the surface
6. Optional: Crystals on the edge of the tub (NOT in the water unless water-safe)
7. Set your intention before entering
8. Soak for at least 20 minutes
9. When you drain the tub, visualize what you're releasing going with the water
