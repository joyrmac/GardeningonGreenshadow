## Getting Started

You don't need 40 crystals. Start with 3. Build from there based on what your life actually calls for. A single tumbled stone held with deep intention is more powerful than a shelf full of specimens you never touch.

**Crystals are tools for focusing intention.** They support mindfulness and spiritual practice — they don't replace medical, psychological, or professional care.

## How to Choose a Crystal

### Method 1: Intuition

Walk through a crystal shop (or scroll online) and notice which stones your eyes are drawn to. Pick them up. Notice physical sensations — tingling, warmth, calm, a magnetic pull. If you're drawn to a stone that doesn't "logically" match your intention, trust it. Your subconscious often knows what you need better than your conscious mind.

### Method 2: Intention

Get clear on what you need support with. Match your intention to a stone's properties (use the tables above). Select by chakra if you know which energy center feels blocked.

### Method 3: Both (Recommended)

Research 3-5 stones that match your intention. Then hold or look at each and let your intuition make the final choice. Logic narrows the options; intuition makes the pick.

### Programming Your Crystal

1. Cleanse the stone first (see below)
2. Hold it in your dominant hand
3. Close your eyes, breathe deeply
4. Visualize your intention vividly — the outcome, the feeling
5. State: *"I program this crystal with the intention of [specific goal]."*
6. Done. Every time you see it, it reinforces your intention.

## How to Cleanse Crystals

Crystals absorb energy. Cleansing resets them. Think of it like clearing your browser history — removing accumulated data so the stone can work fresh.

### Universal Methods (Safe for ALL Crystals)

| Method | How | Duration |
|--------|-----|----------|
| **Moonlight** | Place on windowsill or outdoors under the moon. Full Moon is most powerful for release; New Moon for fresh starts. | Overnight (6-12 hours) |
| **Smoke** | Pass stone through sage, palo santo, or incense smoke for 30-60 seconds, turning to cover all sides. | 30-60 seconds |
| **Sound** | Use a singing bowl, bell, tuning fork, or your own voice. Sound vibrations clear energy. | 1-5 minutes |
| **Selenite** | Place crystals on or beside a selenite plate/wand. Selenite cleanses and charges simultaneously. | 4-24 hours |
| **Breath** | Hold the crystal, take a deep breath, blow gently across it with the intention to clear. | 30 seconds |
| **Brown rice** | Bury crystal in dry uncooked brown rice. Discard rice after (don't eat it). | 24-48 hours |

### Additional Methods (Check Water/Sun Safety First)

| Method | How | Only For |
|--------|-----|----------|
| **Running water** | Hold under cool running water 1-2 minutes. Natural sources (stream, river) are most powerful. | Water-safe crystals only |
| **Salt water** | Dissolve sea salt in water, submerge stone 1-4 hours. Rinse in fresh water after. | Hard, water-safe crystals only |
| **Sunlight** | Place in direct sun for 30 min – 2 hours MAX. Powerful but can damage certain stones. | Sun-safe crystals only |
| **Earth burial** | Bury in clean soil for 24 hours to several days. Great for grounding stones. | All crystals (mark the spot!) |

### How Often to Cleanse

- **Weekly:** Crystals used daily for protection or healing
- **Monthly:** Stones used for meditation or occasional ritual
- **After every use:** Crystals used in readings or healing sessions for others
- **When it feels "off":** Trust your instincts — if the stone feels heavy, dull, or different, cleanse it

## Crystal Care: Water and Sun Safety

### Water Safety Quick Rule

**Crystals ending in "-ite" are generally NOT water safe.** When in doubt, keep it dry.

**SAFE in water (Mohs hardness 6+):**
- Clear Quartz, Amethyst, Rose Quartz, Citrine, Smoky Quartz
- Tiger's Eye, Carnelian, Red Jasper, Black Obsidian
- Bloodstone, Agate varieties (including Blue Lace Agate)

**NEVER put in water:**

| Crystal | Reason |
|---------|--------|
| Selenite | Dissolves (gypsum-based) |
| Malachite | Contains copper — toxic in water |
| Pyrite | Rusts (iron content) |
| Hematite | Rusts (iron content) |
| Fluorite | Fractures, dissolves |
| Lepidolite | Becomes brittle |
| Lapis Lazuli | Contains calcite (dissolves) and pyrite (toxic) |
| Kyanite | Microfractures |
| Moonstone | Irreversible damage |
| Celestite | Dissolves, fragile |
| Calcite (all colors) | Dissolves |
| Howlite | Prolonged exposure damages surface |
| Turquoise | Cracks, loses color |

### Sunlight Sensitivity

**Crystals that FADE in sunlight (limit to 30 minutes or keep in shade):**
- Amethyst, Rose Quartz, Smoky Quartz, Fluorite
- Celestite, Aventurine, Opal, Kunzite

**Crystals that LOVE sunlight:**
- Sunstone, Tiger's Eye, Carnelian, Black Tourmaline
- Clear Quartz, Red Jasper, Bloodstone, Citrine (natural)

### Crystal Grids

A geometric arrangement of crystals that amplifies a specific intention.

**How to build one:**
1. Set your intention clearly
2. Choose a geometry (circle, triangle, Seed of Life, Flower of Life)
3. Select crystals: center stone (primary intention) + supporting stones + outer ring (amplifiers)
4. Place from outside in; center stone goes last
5. Activate by tracing connecting lines with a Clear Quartz point or your finger
6. Leave in place for 48 hours to 40 days

## General Care Tips

- **Dust regularly** with a soft, dry cloth
- **Store** in natural materials: wood boxes, silk or cotton pouches (avoid plastic and metal)
- **Handle soft stones gently:** Selenite, Celestite, Calcite, and Fluorite scratch easily
- **Cleanse after purchase** and after someone else handles your crystals
- **Let go** of crystals that no longer resonate — gift them, bury them, or return them to nature
- **Size doesn't equal power.** A $3 tumbled stone held with deep intention outperforms a $300 specimen sitting forgotten on a shelf.
