# Master Correspondence Table
### A Quick-Reference for Connecting Intentions to Herbs, Crystals, Colors, Moon Phases, and Elements

---

## How to Use This Table

When you're setting an intention — for a ritual, candle, bath, altar setup, or spell jar — this table tells you what to reach for. Everything is organized by **intention** so you start with what you WANT and find the tools that align.

**You don't need all of these for any single working.** Pick 2-3 correspondences that feel right. More isn't stronger — clearer intention is.

## Moon Phase Quick Reference

| Phase | Symbol | Energy | Best For |
|-------|--------|--------|----------|
| **New Moon** | 🌑 | Beginnings, planting seeds | Setting intentions, starting new practices, quiet reflection |
| **Waxing Crescent** | 🌒 | Building, momentum | Taking first action steps, commitment |
| **First Quarter** | 🌓 | Decision, push through | Facing challenges, decisive action |
| **Waxing Gibbous** | 🌔 | Refining, trust | Fine-tuning, patience, staying the course |
| **Full Moon** | 🌕 | Peak, illumination, release | Charging tools, divination, celebration, letting go of what's complete |
| **Waning Gibbous** | 🌖 | Gratitude, sharing | Teaching, giving back, processing what the full moon revealed |
| **Last Quarter** | 🌗 | Releasing, forgiving | Breaking habits, ending what no longer serves |
| **Dark Moon** | 🌘 | Rest, inner work | Deep rest, shadow work, inner stillness, no new beginnings |

**Your power phase:** Full Moon (born under one). You'll feel the most alive, emotional, and magically potent at each Full Moon.

## How to Use This in Practice

**Building a ritual:** Pick your intention → Find the row → Choose 1 herb, 1 crystal, 1 color. Light a candle in that color. Hold the crystal. Burn or brew the herb. Done.

**Dressing a candle:** Choose candle color by intention → anoint with corresponding essential oil → roll in crushed corresponding herb → set intention → light.

**Making a spell jar:** Small jar + corresponding herb + crystal chip + written intention on paper → seal with candle wax in the corresponding color.

**Choosing a ritual day:** Match the day of the week to the planetary energy of your intention.

**Timing by moon:** Waxing = attract/build. Waning = release/banish. Full = charge/celebrate. New = plant seeds/set intentions.
