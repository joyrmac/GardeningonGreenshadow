# Moon Phase Tracker & Ritual Journal
### A Monthly Template for Tracking Lunar Cycles, Intentions, and Growth

---

## How to Use This Journal

The moon completes a full cycle approximately every 29.5 days, moving through eight phases. Each phase carries a different energy. Tracking these phases — and your own patterns alongside them — builds a powerful self-awareness practice over time.

**Joy, a note for you specifically:** You were born under a Full Moon (Virgo Sun opposite Pisces Moon). This means the Full Moon phase is your power phase each month — you'll likely feel the most energized, emotional, and creatively alive during this time. Pay extra attention to how you feel at each Full Moon. The New Moon is your time for deep rest and quiet intention-setting.

---

## The Eight Moon Phases

| Phase | Symbol | Energy | Practice |
|-------|--------|--------|----------|
| **New Moon** | 🌑 | Planting seeds, fresh starts, quiet intention | Set intentions, journal what you want to manifest |
| **Waxing Crescent** | 🌒 | Building momentum, hope, commitment | Take first action steps toward your intentions |
| **First Quarter** | 🌓 | Challenge, decision, push through | Face obstacles head-on, recommit |
| **Waxing Gibbous** | 🌔 | Refinement, patience, adjustment | Fine-tune your approach, be patient |
| **Full Moon** | 🌕 | Peak energy, illumination, release | Release what's not serving you, celebrate wins |
| **Waning Gibbous** | 🌖 | Gratitude, sharing, teaching | Share what you've learned, express thanks |
| **Third Quarter** | 🌗 | Forgiveness, letting go, clearing | Forgive, declutter (physical and emotional) |
| **Waning Crescent** | 🌘 | Rest, surrender, preparation | Rest deeply, prepare for new cycle |

## Tracking Your Personal Moon Patterns

Over time, notice:

- **Which moon sign do you feel most energized during?** (When the moon is in a fire sign? Water sign? Your own sign?)
- **Do you sleep differently around the Full Moon?**
- **When do your best ideas come?** (Waxing or waning phase?)
- **When do conflicts tend to arise?** (First Quarter is common)
- **When do you feel most creative?** (Waxing Gibbous and Full Moon are common)
- **When do you feel most drained?** (Waning Crescent is common)

**Joy's moon patterns to watch for specifically:**
- Full Moons in **Pisces** (your natal Moon sign) — the most emotionally intense Full Moons for you each year
- Full Moons in **Virgo** (your natal Sun sign) — illumination around identity, purpose, and self-expression
- New Moons in **Leo** (your stellium) — powerful times for setting creative and career intentions
- New Moons in **Libra** (your rising sign) — fresh starts in how you present yourself to the world

## Quick Reference: Moon Phase Rituals

### New Moon Ritual (5-10 minutes)
1. Light a candle in a dark room
2. Write your intentions for the cycle
3. Read them out loud
4. Place paper on altar or in a journal
5. Sit in silence, visualize intentions fulfilled
6. Blow out candle

### Full Moon Ritual (5-10 minutes)
1. Light a candle (or sit in moonlight)
2. Write what you're releasing on paper
3. Read it out loud with conviction
4. Safely burn the paper or tear it up and throw it away
5. Write 3 things you're grateful for
6. Drink a glass of water as an act of self-care
7. Blow out candle
