## CARD 8: Safety Don'ts (Post in Bathroom/Kitchen)

```
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                        SAFETY DON'Ts
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

❌ DON'T mix with bleach or ammonia
   → Creates TOXIC GAS (dangerous!)
   → Use oils OR bleach, never both

❌ DON'T apply undiluted to kids' skin
   → Always dilute (0.5-1% for children)
   → Skin is more permeable in kids

❌ DON'T ingest without guidance
   → Oils are NOT vitamins
   → Internal use requires professional guidance

❌ DON'T leave diffuser running unattended
   → Especially around young children/pets
   → Use timer, take breaks

❌ DON'T assume "natural = safe for everyone"
   → Individual sensitivities vary
   → Always patch test new oils

❌ DON'T store in plastic
   → Oils degrade plastic
   → Use glass bottles only

✅ DO ask questions when unsure
✅ DO patch test before first use
✅ DO dilute properly
✅ DO take breaks from continuous diffusion
✅ DO store properly (cool, dark, glass)

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
Poison Control: 1-800-222-1222
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
```

## CARD 9: Diffusion 101

```
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                    HOW TO DIFFUSE OILS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

🌊 WATER DIFFUSER (Most Common)
   1. Fill with filtered/distilled water
   2. Add essential oil drops (per recipe)
   3. Turn on
   4. Diffuse 20-30 minutes
   5. Take break before running again
   6. Clean regularly per diffuser instructions

⏰ TIMING GUIDELINES
   • 20-30 min per session
   • Take 15-30 min break between sessions
   • Don't run continuously
   • Shorter is better than longer

💧 DROP COUNT QUICK GUIDE
   • Small diffuser (< 100ml): 2-4 drops
   • Medium diffuser (100-300ml): 3-5 drops
   • Large diffuser (> 300ml): 5-8 drops

🌬️  DIFFUSER PLACEMENT
   ✅ Central location (bedroom, living room, kitchen)
   ✅ Table/shelf height (0-3 feet high)
   ❌ On floor (oils don't disperse well)
   ❌ In corners (dead air zones)
   ❌ Near vents (oils get blown away)

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
```
