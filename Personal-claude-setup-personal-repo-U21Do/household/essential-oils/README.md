## 🛡️ Essential Safety Rules (Summary)

### **The 5 Golden Rules:**

1. **Always Dilute** for topical use
   - Adults: 2-5%
   - Teens: 1-2%
   - Children: 0.5-1%
   - See [safety-guide.md](resources/safety-guide.md) for details

2. **Know Your Child's Safe Oils**
   - Murphy (8): Lavender, Frankincense, Sweet Orange, Cedarwood
   - Birdie (10): Above + Rosemary, Clary Sage (diluted)
   - Chad (16): Most oils with teen dilution

3. **Never Mix with Bleach or Ammonia**
   - Creates toxic gas
   - Use oils OR bleach/ammonia, never both

4. **Patch Test New Oils**
   - Apply small diluted amount
   - Wait 24 hours
   - Watch for redness/itching

5. **Start Low & Go Slow**
   - Begin with lower concentrations
   - Increase only if well-tolerated
   - Especially important for children & Joy (PTSD/anxiety)

## 🌟 Tips for Success

### **What Works Well:**
1. **Consistency over perfection** - same blend same time = powerful
2. **Start simple** - 2-3 oils work better than complex blends
3. **Individual preference matters** - what works for one may not for another
4. **Pair with routine** - blend + activity = stronger effect
5. **Track it** - journal helps you see patterns over time

### **Common Pitfalls to Avoid:**
- ❌ Over-complicating blends (more oils ≠ better)
- ❌ Changing blends too often (consistency builds effectiveness)
- ❌ Not diluting properly (safety first!)
- ❌ Forgetting to take breaks (desensitization happens)
- ❌ Forcing family members to use blends (autonomy matters)

### **Troubleshooting:**
- **Blend not working?** Try simpler formula, different carrier oil, or different application method
- **Family member resistant?** Ask about preference, try different blends, respect choice
- **Not seeing results?** Give it 2-3 weeks of consistent use; track in journal
- **Cost concerns?** Homemade products cost 70-95% less than commercial equivalents
