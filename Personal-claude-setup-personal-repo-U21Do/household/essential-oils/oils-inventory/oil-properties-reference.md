## Plant Therapy Singles — Top 14 Set

### **Clary Sage**
- **Scent Profile:** Herbal, slightly sweet, earthy
- **Therapeutic Properties:** Calming, grounding, hormone-balancing, mood-lifting
- **Best For:** Stress relief, emotional clarity, creative blocks, anxiety
- **Family Uses:** Joy (ADHD focus), Murphy (perfectionism interrupt), Chad (emotional processing)

### **Bergamot**
- **Scent Profile:** Citrus, floral, uplifting, energetic

- **Family Uses:** Joy (ADHD energy dip), Chad (emotional lift), family Monday blues

## Safe Dilution Guidelines for the McNamara Family

### **For Diffusion (no dilution needed)**
- Use undiluted drops in diffuser
- Child-safe: All oils marked "KidSafe" plus Lavender, Frankincense, Sweet Orange, Orange

### **For Topical Application (body oils, lotions, creams)**

| Age Group | Dilution % | Ratio | Example |
|-----------|-----------|-------|---------|
| **Joy (adult)** | 2-5% | 2-5 drops per 1 tsp carrier oil | Anxiety blend: 3 drops lavender + 2 drops frankincense in 1 tsp coconut oil |
| **Sean (adult)** | 2-5% | 2-5 drops per 1 tsp carrier oil | Energy blend for work |
| **Chad (teen 16)** | 1-2% | 1-2 drops per 1 tsp carrier oil | Emotional support blend |
| **Birdie (10)** | 0.5-1% | 1 drop per 1 tsp carrier oil | Anxiety/stomach support |
| **Murphy (8)** | 0.5-1% | 1 drop per 1 tsp carrier oil | Bedtime support, calming blend |

### **Safe Single Oils for Direct Skin Application (diluted)**
- Lavender: 1-3%
- Frankincense: 1-3%
- Chamomile: 1-2% (if available)
- Tea Tree: 1% (diluted, not for children under 2)
- Peppermint: 0.5-1% (not for infants)

### **Oils Requiring Extra Caution**
- **Cinnamon Cassia:** Skin irritant - dilute heavily (0.5% max), patch test
- **Bergamot:** Photosensitizing - do NOT use on exposed skin before sun
- **Tea Tree:** Keep away from mouths; can irritate if overused
- **Peppermint:** Stimulating - avoid evening use, can overstimulate if too concentrated

## Storage & Care

- **Keep in:** Cool, dark place (not direct sunlight)
- **Containers:** Amber/dark glass bottles with tight caps
- **Shelf Life:** Most last 1-2 years; check before each use
- **Signs of Degradation:** Changed color, rancid smell, separation of oils
- **Temperature:** Below 70°F ideal; away from heat sources
