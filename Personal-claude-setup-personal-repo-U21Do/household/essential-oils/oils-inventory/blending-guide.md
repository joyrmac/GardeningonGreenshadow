## Blending Basics

### **The Blending "Rule of 4"**
Most harmonious blends use **4 or fewer oils** to start. This allows individual oils to shine while creating cohesion.

- **Simple blend:** 2 oils
- **Classic blend:** 3 oils
- **Complex blend:** 4 oils
- **Advanced:** 5+ oils (save for experienced blenders)

### **The Three "Notes" System**

Essential oils have a scent "note" based on how quickly they evaporate and how their scent develops:

| Note | Evaporation | Character | Examples from Your Collection |
|------|-------------|-----------|-------------------------------|
| **Top Note** | Fast (5-15 min) | Bright, initial impact, energizing | Lemon, Peppermint, Bergamot, Spearmint, Citrus |
| **Middle Note** | Medium (30 min - 2 hours) | Heart of blend, balanced, rounded | Rosemary, Lavender, Ylang Ylang, Clary Sage, Basil |
| **Base Note** | Slow (2+ hours) | Grounding, lasting, deepening | Frankincense, Cedarwood, Patchouli, Vetiver, Cinnamon |

### **How to Use Notes in Blending**

A balanced blend typically includes:

- **30% Top Notes** (brightness, opening impact)
- **50% Middle Notes** (body, character, heart)
- **20% Base Notes** (grounding, lasting power)

**Example: "Clarity & Calm" Blend**
- 2 drops Lemon (top) = 30%
- 3 drops Lavender (middle) = 50%
- 1 drop Frankincense (base) = 20%
- **Total: 6 drops** for diffuser

## How to Create Your Own Blends

### **Step 1: Define Your Purpose**
- What emotion/state do you want to create?
- Who is this for?
- When will it be used?

### **Step 2: Choose Your Notes**
- Select 1-2 top notes (brightness, opening)
- Select 1-2 middle notes (heart, balance)
- Select 1 base note (grounding, lasting)

### **Step 3: Follow the Ratio**
Use drops to create approximately:
- 30% top notes
- 50% middle notes
- 20% base notes

**Example: 6-drop blend**
- 2 drops top note (30%)
- 3 drops middle note (50%)
- 1 drop base note (20%)

### **Step 4: Test Scent Profile**
For **diffuser blends:**
1. Add oils to diffuser with water
2. Run for 10 minutes
3. Note initial scent (top notes)
4. Return in 30 minutes (check heart notes)
5. Check again at 2 hours (base notes developing)

For **topical blends:**
1. Add to carrier oil in small amount
2. Smell immediately
3. Wait 5 minutes (middle notes)
4. Wait 30 minutes (base notes)
5. Adjust concentrations if needed for next batch

### **Step 5: Adjust & Record**
- Did it work as intended?
- Any adjustments needed?
- Record recipe for future use
- Rate effectiveness on scale

## Blending Tips & Tricks

### **"Fix It" Techniques**

**Blend is too strong?**
- Add more carrier oil (topical) or diffuse for shorter time
- Add a lighter top note to balance (Lemon, Bergamot)
- Next time: Reduce drops overall

**Blend is too subtle?**
- Use more drops in diffuser or increase carrier oil concentration (topical)
- Add a stronger base note (Frankincense, Cedarwood, Vetiver)
- Next time: Increase overall drops or use fewer carrier oil drops

**Blend is too "flowery"?**
- Add fresh/citrus top notes (Lemon, Spearmint, Bergamot)
- Add wood/earth base notes (Cedarwood, Vetiver, Frankincense)
- Next time: Balance floral middle notes with more top/base notes

**Blend is too "sharp"?**
- Add softer middle notes (Lavender, Ylang Ylang, Clary Sage)
- Add grounding base notes (Frankincense, Cedarwood)
- Next time: Reduce citrus/mint top notes

### **Layering Technique**
Create depth by using multiple products:
1. **Diffuser blend** (shared space, lighter)
2. **Personal topical blend** (diluted oil on wrists)
3. **Bath blend** (mixed in carrier oil, added to bath)
4. **Ritual blend** (specific purpose - bedtime, meditation)

### **Seasonal Blending**
- **Spring:** Lighter, fresher top notes (Lemon, Bergamot, Spearmint)
- **Summer:** Cooling middle notes (Peppermint, Eucalyptus, cooling florals)
- **Fall:** Warm, spicy base notes (Cinnamon, Orange, Frankincense)
- **Winter:** Deep grounding (Cedarwood, Frankincense, Vetiver)

### **Mood-Based Blending**
- **Energized:** More top notes, less base
- **Calm:** More base notes, gentle middle
- **Focused:** Balance of top (clarity) + middle (stability) + base (grounding)
- **Creative:** More middle notes, uplifting top notes, grounding base

## Safety Reminders During Blending

- ✅ Always dilute properly for topical use
- ✅ Patch test new blends (especially children)
- ✅ Label all blends clearly (especially important with kids)
- ✅ Store blends in dark glass bottles in cool place
- ✅ Keep sharp/hot oils away from children's reach
- ❌ Do NOT ingest oils without proper training
- ❌ Do NOT apply undiluted to children's skin
- ❌ Do NOT leave diffuser running unattended
- ❌ Do NOT assume "natural" = "safe for everyone"
