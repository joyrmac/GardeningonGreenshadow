## 🔍 Data Analysis Tips

**Look for Patterns:**
- Which blends get most use?
- Which family member benefits most?
- Best time of day for use?
- Does consistency matter?
- Does pairing with activity help?

**Calculate Real Impact:**
- Sleep improvement in minutes
- Anxiety level reduction
- Perfectionism spiral frequency change
- Family cooperation on chore days

**Use Data to Make Decisions:**
- Which oils to reorder
- Which blends to create regularly
- Which blends to retire
- What to gift to friends
