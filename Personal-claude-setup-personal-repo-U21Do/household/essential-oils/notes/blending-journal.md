# 📖 Blending Experiment Journal
*Track Your Essential Oil Discoveries*

> Use this space to document what you create, what works, what doesn't, and what you learn about your family's preferences and needs.

---

## How to Use This Journal

### **For Each Blend, Record:**
1. **Date created** - when you made it
2. **Blend name** - what you're calling it
3. **Purpose** - what you're using it for
4. **Oils used** - exact drops/amounts
5. **Who for** - which family member or general use
6. **Initial reaction** - how it smells at first
7. **Development** - how it evolves (middle notes, base notes)
8. **Effectiveness** - did it work as intended?
9. **Rating** - would you recommend it?
10. **Notes** - adjustments, ideas for next time
