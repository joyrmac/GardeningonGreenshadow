## 📋 Cleaning Day Blending Tips

✅ **Match the task:** Different rooms/jobs have ideal blends
✅ **Energize appropriately:** Peppermint for big projects, Lavender for calming focus
✅ **Pair with cleaning products:** Use blend that matches your actual cleaner
✅ **Take breaks:** Don't run diffuser entire cleaning time
✅ **Sunday ritual:** Make family prep time special with consistent blend
✅ **Motivate kids:** Let each child pick their cleaning-day blend (Birdie might pick "Organize & Declutter")

---

## 🎯 Cleaning Day Diffusion Strategy

### **For 1-Hour Cleaning Session:**
1. **First 30 minutes:** Diffuse cleaning motivation blend
2. **Break:** 15 minutes off (let it air out, water the diffuser)
3. **Final 15 minutes:** Refresh diffuser if needed, or turn off

### **For Whole Morning (2-3 hours):**
1. **First hour:** Start with blend
2. **Break:** 30 minutes off
3. **Hour 2-3:** Restart diffuser with fresh load
4. **Final bit:** May not need - depends on energy
