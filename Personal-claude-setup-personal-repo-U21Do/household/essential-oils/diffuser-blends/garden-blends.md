## ACTIVE GARDENING WORK

### **"Active Garden Work Energy"**

- Cedarwood: 1 drop

**When to Use:** During active garden work, planting, building beds

---

### **"Garden Recovery Bath"**
**After physical garden work**

**Recipe:**
- Lavender: 3 drops
- Peppermint: 1 drop
- Frankincense: 1 drop

**How to Use:**
- Add to 2 cups Epsom salt
- Let sit 24 hours to absorb
- Use ½-1 cup in warm bath
- Soak 15-20 minutes

**When to Use:** After physical garden work, sore muscles, recovery time
**Why It Works:** Soothing + energizing refreshment + grounding recovery
**Best For:** Muscle recovery after active gardening

## 🌱 Garden Connection Ritual

Consider using these blends as part of a garden ritual:

**Morning Garden Ritual:**
1. Use "Garden Vision & Planning" blend
2. Walk through all your garden areas
3. Observe what's growing, what needs attention
4. Feel gratitude for your creation

**Harvest Celebration:**
1. Use "Garden Abundance" blend
2. Gather harvests with joy & gratitude
3. Notice beauty of what grew
4. Celebrate your work & nature's partnership

**Seasonal Transition:**
1. Use seasonal blend appropriate to shift
2. Acknowledge what's ending
3. Plant seeds of hope/planning for next season
4. Trust the cycles
