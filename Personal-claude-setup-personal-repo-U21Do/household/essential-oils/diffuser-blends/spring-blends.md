# 🌸 Spring Blends
*March - May | Fresh, Light, Renewal, New Beginnings*

---

## "Spring Awakening"
**The All-Purpose Spring Blend**

**Recipe:**
- Lemon: 2 drops
- Bergamot: 1 drop
- Sweet Orange: 1 drop
- Lavender: 1 drop

**When to Use:** Morning diffusion, spring cleaning time, garden planning season
**Why:** Fresh + uplifting + clean + calming
**Ritual:** Open windows, start this blend, let fresh air in
**Best For:** Whole family, anytime during spring

---

## "Garden Prep Energy"
**For Planning & Planting**

**Recipe:**
- Lemon: 2 drops
- Rosemary: 2 drops
- Frankincense: 1 drop

**When to Use:** Garden planning sessions, bed prep, planting days
**Why:** Mental clarity + energy + grounding
**Best For:** Joy's garden design work, family garden projects
**Family Impact:** Energizing without overstimulating

---

## "Fresh Start"
**New Beginnings & Projects**

**Recipe:**
- Sweet Orange: 2 drops
- Bergamot: 1 drop
- Lemon: 1 drop
- Frankincense: 1 drop

**When to Use:** Starting new projects, spring equinox (March 20-21), fresh start moments
**Why:** Joyful + uplifting + grounding
**Ritual:** Use when launching new initiative or goal
**Celebration:** Pair with intentional beginning ritual

---

## "Spring Cleaning Energy"
**Motivation for the Task**

**Recipe:**
- Lemon: 2 drops
- Peppermint: 1 drop
- Sweet Orange: 1 drop

**When to Use:** Spring cleaning day, organizing projects, big home refresh
**Why:** Energizing + motivating + fresh + uplifting
**Best For:** Making chore time feel lighter
**Duration:** Diffuse during cleaning work

---

## "Spring Renewal Bath"
**Evening Refresh**

**Recipe:**
- Lavender: 3 drops
- Lemon: 2 drops
- Sweet Orange: 1 drop

**How to Use:**
- Add to 2 cups Epsom salt
- Let sit 24 hours to absorb
- Use ½-1 cup in warm bath
- Soak 15-20 minutes

**When to Use:** Evening, spring equinox, after active spring day
**Why:** Fresh + calming + joyful wind-down
**Best For:** Transition from active spring days to evening rest

---

## "Spring Balance"
**Equinox Blend**

**Recipe:**
- Lavender: 2 drops
- Frankincense: 2 drops
- Lemon: 1 drop

**When to Use:** Spring equinox (March 20-21), balance-seeking moments
**Why:** Grounding + fresh + calm = equilibrium
**Ritual:** Use during meditation, yoga, or quiet reflection
**Significance:** Honors equal day/night balance of equinox

---

## "April Showers"
**Rainy Day Blend**

**Recipe:**
- Lemon: 2 drops
- Lavender: 2 drops
- Frankincense: 1 drop

**When to Use:** Rainy spring days, when needing calm indoors
**Why:** Bright + calm + grounding on gray days
**Best For:** Kids cooped up on rainy days
**Mood Support:** Lifts mood while respecting weather

---

## 📋 Spring Blending Tips

✅ **Layer your blends:** Start with "Spring Awakening" in morning, shift to "Fresh Start" for afternoon projects
✅ **Match activity:** "Garden Prep Energy" for planning, "Spring Cleaning Energy" for chores
✅ **Celebrate equinox:** Use "Spring Balance" on March 20-21
✅ **Honor the season:** Let spring's lightness guide your diffusion time
✅ **Support transitions:** Easy spring transition after winter darkness
