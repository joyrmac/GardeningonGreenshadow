## How to Use These Recipes

### **For Water Diffusers:**
1. Fill diffuser with filtered or distilled water
2. Add drops of essential oils as specified
3. Run for 30 minutes to 1 hour (or follow diffuser instructions)
4. Take breaks between diffusions (15 min on, 15 min off is ideal)

### **For Nebulizing Diffusers:**
- Use oils straight (no water needed)
- Use same drop count as water diffuser recipes
- Typically very effective; more concentrated

### **For Car/Personal Diffusers:**
- Scale recipes down to 2-3 drops total
- Refresh as scent fades
