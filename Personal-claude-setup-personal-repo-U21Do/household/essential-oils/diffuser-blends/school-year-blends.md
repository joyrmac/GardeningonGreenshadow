## 💡 School Year Tips

✅ **Consistency matters:** Same blend for homework = strong routine
✅ **Start early:** Begin blend 10-15 min before activity
✅ **Transition prep:** Use special blend for any new challenge
✅ **Meet them where they are:** Adjust blend if anxiety is high
✅ **Celebrate wins:** Use "performance confidence" before good results
✅ **Respect learning styles:** Each kid benefits from different blends

## 🏆 Success Metrics to Track

- **Focus improvements:** How long can each child focus?
- **Anxiety reduction:** Rate before/after blend
- **School attitude:** More positive, less resistance?

- **Homework ease:** Less conflict, faster completion?
