# ❄️ Winter Blends
*December - February | Deep, Grounding, Magical, Spiritual, Warm, Cozy*

## "Winter Grounding & Magic" ⭐
**The Season-Long Essential**

**Recipe:**
- Cedarwood: 2 drops
- Frankincense: 2 drops
- Cinnamon Cassia: 1 drop
- Sweet Orange: 1 drop

**When to Use:** December through February, use consistently all season
**Why:** Deep grounding + spiritual + warm + celebratory
**Best For:** Primary winter blend for family nervous system support
**Ritual:** Use daily December-February for steady foundation
**Family Impact:** Everyone benefits from deep grounding through dark months
**Pro Tip:** This becomes your "winter ritual" - consistency is power

## 🌙 Winter Seasonal Affective Disorder (SAD) Support

If family members struggle with winter darkness:

**Morning Ritual:**
- Use "Winter Calm & Clarity" (Rosemary + Lemon + Frankincense)
- 20-30 min diffusion in morning
- Pairs with morning light exposure

**Midday Boost:**
- Same blend or refresh with new diffuser load
- Support focus & energy through gray days

**Evening Grounding:**
- Switch to "Winter Grounding & Magic" or "Cozy Winter Night"
- Support nervous system transition to rest
