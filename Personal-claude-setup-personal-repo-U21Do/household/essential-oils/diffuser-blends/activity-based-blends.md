## 💡 Pro Tips for Activity Blends

✅ **Start early:** Begin blend 10-15 minutes before activity starts
✅ **Consistency:** Use same blend for same activity = stronger association
✅ **Ritual:** Blend becomes signal that activity is starting
✅ **Respect autonomy:** Let Chad/kids choose if they want blend
✅ **Adjust timing:** Shift blend based on actual activity time
✅ **Season shifts:** Adjust carrier/intensity based on season
