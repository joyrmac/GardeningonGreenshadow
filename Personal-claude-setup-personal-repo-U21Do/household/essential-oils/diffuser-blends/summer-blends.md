# 🌞 Summer Blends
*June - August | Active, Energizing, Cooling, Bright*

---

## "Summer Garden Energy"
**The Active Projects Blend**

**Recipe:**
- Peppermint: 2 drops
- Lemon: 2 drops
- Eucalyptus: 1 drop

**When to Use:** Garden work, outdoor projects, physical activity time
**Why:** Energizing + cooling + clarity
**Best For:** Active family projects, yard work, high-energy tasks
**⚠️ Note:** Energizing - use during day, not evening
**Family Impact:** Motivates without hyperactivity

---

## "Bright & Breezy"
**Light & Airy Daytime**

**Recipe:**
- Lemon: 2 drops
- Sweet Orange: 2 drops
- Bergamot: 1 drop

**When to Use:** Morning, midday, any time you want cheerful atmosphere
**Why:** Bright + joyful + light + uplifting
**Best For:** Creating happy summer mood
**Family Impact:** Everyone responds to brightness

---

## "Cooling Afternoon"
**Heat Relief (2-4 PM Slump)**

**Recipe:**
- Peppermint: 2 drops
- Eucalyptus: 1 drop
- Lemon: 2 drops

**When to Use:** Hot afternoon, before outdoor activity, cooling down
**Why:** Cooling + refreshing + energizing without heaviness
**Best For:** Murphy's afternoon energy dip, family heat relief
**Pro Tip:** Pair with cold drink and shaded outdoor time

---

## "Summer BBQ & Gathering"
**Party & Gathering Blend**

**Recipe:**
- Sweet Orange: 2 drops
- Bergamot: 1 drop
- Lemon: 2 drops

**When to Use:** Family gatherings, barbecues, porch parties, guests arriving
**Why:** Festive + fresh + inviting + joyful
**Ritual:** Start 30 minutes before guests arrive
**Family Impact:** Sets welcoming, happy tone

---

## "Evening Cool Down"
**Sunset Transition**

**Recipe:**
- Lavender: 3 drops
- Peppermint: 1 drop
- Frankincense: 1 drop

**When to Use:** Late afternoon/early evening, as heat cools and day winds down
**Why:** Transition from activity + cooling + early wind-down signal
**Best For:** Late summer evening outdoor family time
**Ritual:** Shift into evening mode while still enjoying outdoors

---

## "Summer Fruit Paradise"
**Joyful, Fruity, Fun**

**Recipe:**
- Sweet Orange: 2 drops
- Lemon: 1 drop
- Bergamot: 1 drop
- Ylang Ylang: 1 drop

**When to Use:** Summer fun time, celebrations, joyful moments
**Why:** Fruity + joyful + sensual + bright
**Best For:** Celebrating summer abundance, Murphy's creative play time
**Family Impact:** Pure joy & celebration energy

---

## "Poolside Refresh"
**At the Water**

**Recipe:**
- Peppermint: 2 drops
- Lemon: 2 drops
- Sweet Orange: 1 drop

**When to Use:** By the pool or water, before swimming, outdoor water play
**Why:** Cooling + refreshing + energizing + summery
**Best For:** Making water time feel even better
**Ritual:** Diffuse near pool/seating area during water time

---

## 📋 Summer Blending Tips

✅ **Cooling is key:** Peppermint + Eucalyptus help combat heat
✅ **Early morning start:** Uplifting blends set positive tone
✅ **Afternoon energy:** Use cooling blends for 2-4 PM slump
✅ **Evening transition:** Cooling blends help shift from heat to evening
✅ **Outdoor gathering:** Start "Summer BBQ" blend before guests arrive
✅ **Avoid evening stimulation:** No high-energy blends after 6 PM
