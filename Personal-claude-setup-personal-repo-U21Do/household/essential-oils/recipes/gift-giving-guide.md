# 🎁 Gift-Giving Guide
*Share Your Essential Oils & Blends with Loved Ones*

---

## SIMPLE GIFT IDEAS

### **1. Diffuser Blend Gift Set** ⭐ Easiest
**What You Need:**
- 3-4 of your favorite blends (written on pretty cards)
- Small bottles or containers
- Instruction cards with drops & usage

**How to Assemble:**
1. Choose 3-4 complementary blends
2. Write recipe on decorative card
3. Include "How to Use" instructions
4. Pack in small gift box
5. Add ribbon

**Best For:** Anyone on your list
**Cost:** $2-5 per set
**Effort:** Low

---

### **2. Bath Salts Gift**
**What You Need:**
- Epsom salt (bulk)
- 3-4 oils
- Glass jars or containers
- Labels

**How to Assemble:**
1. Make 2-3 different scent combinations
2. Put in pretty jars with labels
3. Include bath water amount instructions
4. Add decorative lid/label

**Best For:** Relaxation-focused people
**Cost:** $3-7 per jar
**Effort:** Medium

---

### **3. Personal Oil Roller Bottle**
**What You Need:**
- Empty roller bottle (on Amazon)
- Carrier oil (jojoba or sweet almond)
- 6-8 essential oil drops
- Label

**How to Assemble:**
1. Fill roller bottle with carrier oil (¾ full)
2. Add drops of blended oils
3. Cap and label
4. Wrap with ribbon

**Best For:** People who like pulse-point application
**Cost:** $4-8 per bottle
**Effort:** Low

---

### **4. Body Butter or Lotion Gift**
**What You Need:**
- Base recipe ingredients (shea butter, coconut oil)
- Essential oils
- Small glass jars
- Labels

**How to Assemble:**
1. Make small batches of body butter
2. Choose 2-3 signature scents
3. Put in small jars
4. Label with ingredients & scent description
5. Pack in gift box with ribbon

**Best For:** Self-care lovers
**Cost:** $5-10 per jar
**Effort:** Medium-High

---

### **5. Inhaler Gift** (Portable & Practical)
**What You Need:**
- Blank essential oil inhalers (buy on Amazon)
- Selection of oils
- Decorative labels

**How to Assemble:**
1. Create 2-3 different blends
2. Fill inhalers (add 8-10 drops per wick)
3. Label each with purpose (Sleep, Calm, Focus)
4. Package in small box
5. Include instruction card

**Best For:** People who want portable aromatherapy
**Cost:** $8-15 per set (3 inhalers)
**Effort:** Low

---

### **6. Cleaning Supplies Gift Set**
**What You Need:**
- Your homemade cleaner recipes (all-purpose, glass, etc.)
- Small spray bottles
- Labels
- Box for packaging

**How to Assemble:**
1. Make 2-3 different cleaning blends
2. Put in spray bottles
3. Label clearly ("All-Purpose," "Glass & Mirror," etc.)
4. Include usage instructions
5. Market as "Natural Cleaning Package"

**Best For:** Eco-conscious, practical gift-givers
**Cost:** $5-10 per set
**Effort:** Medium

## PACKAGING IDEAS

### **Budget-Friendly Wrapping:**
- Kraft paper + twine
- Newspaper comic section + ribbon
- Plain brown paper + dried flowers
- Fabric scrap + decorated label

### **Gift Box Assembly:**
1. Choose small attractive box
2. Line with tissue paper or shredded paper
3. Arrange items artfully
4. Add decorative elements (dried flowers, ribbons)
5. Tuck in instruction card
6. Wrap or tie with ribbon

### **Presentation Cards:**
Create cards that include:
- Blend name
- Recipe (so they can make more!)
- Why you chose this for them
- How to use it
- Small personal message

## MAKE-AHEAD TIMELINE

**2 Weeks Before:**
- [ ] Decide on gift ideas
- [ ] Gather all supplies
- [ ] Source any missing items (inhalers, bottles, jars)

**1 Week Before:**
- [ ] Make bath salts (they need 24 hours to absorb oils)
- [ ] Make body butters (they need time to set)
- [ ] Make any roller bottles

**3-4 Days Before:**
- [ ] Create diffuser blend cards
- [ ] Make room sprays (shorter shelf life)
- [ ] Design instruction cards
- [ ] Gather packaging supplies

**2 Days Before:**
- [ ] Assemble all gifts
- [ ] Wrap gifts
- [ ] Write personal notes

**Day Before/Day Of:**
- [ ] Final preparations
- [ ] Delivery ready

## TEACHING PEOPLE ABOUT YOUR GIFTS

**Include with Every Gift:**

✓ **Blend name** - "Murphy's Bedtime Ritual"
✓ **Recipe** - So they can make more
✓ **Purpose** - Why this blend
✓ **How to use** - Clear instructions
✓ **Safety notes** - If any (dilution, age-appropriate, etc.)
✓ **Shelf life** - How long it lasts
✓ **Personal note** - Why you chose it for them
