## Bath Salts

*Use for: relaxation, sore muscles, anxiety relief, sleep support*

### **"Murphy's Bedtime Bath Soak"** ⭐ **PRIORITY**

**Recipe:**
- 2 cups Epsom salt
- 10 drops Lavender essential oil
- 5 drops Relax™ Blend (Plant Therapy)
- 3 drops Frankincense
- Glass jar with lid
- Fork or spoon

**Directions:**
1. Pour Epsom salt into jar
2. Add oils drop by drop
3. Stir thoroughly with fork, breaking up clumps
4. Let sit 24 hours for oils to fully absorb
5. Use: Add ½-1 cup to warm bath water
6. Stir water to distribute salts
7. Soak for 15-20 minutes
8. Perfect 30-45 minutes before bedtime

**Shelf Life:** 2-3 months
**Storage:** Glass jar in cool, dark place
**For Murphy:** This becomes part of her bedtime routine, signaling wind-down
**Alternative:** Use without Relax™ Blend if unavailable; Lavender + Frankincense is sufficient

## Bath Oils (Carrier Oil Base)

*Use for: moisturizing, massage, gentle warmth*

### **"Murphy's Sleep Support Bath Oil"**

**Recipe:**
- ½ cup coconut oil (or sweet almond oil for sensitive skin)
- 6 drops Lavender
- 4 drops Relax™ Blend
- 2 drops Frankincense
- Dark glass bottle

**Directions:**
1. Pour carrier oil into bottle
2. Add oils carefully
3. Shake well to combine
4. Use: Add 1-2 tablespoons to warm bathwater
5. Stir to disperse in water
6. Soak 15-20 minutes

**Shelf Life:** 1-2 months
**Note:** This is DIFFERENT from bath salts; creates slippery bath (supervise children)
**Warning:** Coconut oil can solidify in cold weather; warm bottle in hands first

## Lotion Bars & Body Butters

*Use for: intensive moisturizing, portable, long-lasting*

### **"Simple Moisturizing Body Butter"**

**Recipe:**
- ½ cup coconut oil
- ½ cup shea butter
- 15 drops Lavender
- 5 drops Sweet Orange
- Whipped together in bowl
- Glass jar for storage

**Directions:**
1. Soften coconut oil and shea butter (let sit at room temperature)
2. Mix together with fork until creamy
3. Add oils
4. Whip with electric mixer (optional - creates fluffier texture) or stir well
5. Transfer to jar
6. Let cool and set (about 1 hour)
7. Use: Small amount on hands, rub into skin

**Shelf Life:** 2-3 months
**Best For:** Dry skin, winter months, evening moisturizing
**Texture:** Soft, rich, pleasant scent
**For Kids:** Make unscented or lightly scented version

---

### **"Lavender Honey Body Butter" (Luxe)**

**Recipe:**
- ½ cup shea butter
- ¼ cup coconut oil
- 2 tablespoons raw honey
- 10 drops Lavender
- 2 drops Frankincense
- Glass jar

**Directions:**
1. Soften shea butter and coconut oil
2. Warm honey gently (makes it pourable)
3. Mix oils and honey
4. Whip butters until fluffy
5. Fold in honey-oil mixture
6. Add essential oils
7. Store in glass jar
8. Use: Tablespoon amount; melts on warm skin

**Shelf Life:** 1-2 months (honey can ferment if bacteria present)
**Luxury Feel:** Rich, nourishing, spa-like
**Best For:** Self-care evenings, dry patches

## Scrubs

*Use for: exfoliation, dead skin removal, aromatherapy*

### **"Lavender-Coconut Body Scrub"**

**Recipe:**
- 1 cup coconut oil
- 1 cup brown sugar (or sea salt)
- 15 drops Lavender
- 5 drops Sweet Orange
- Glass jar
- Fork

**Directions:**
1. Mix brown sugar and oils together thoroughly
2. Break up any clumps with fork
3. Let sit 24 hours for oils to absorb
4. Use: Wet skin in shower, apply ¼ cup scrub
5. Massage gently in circular motions
6. Rinse thoroughly
7. Pat dry and moisturize

**Shelf Life:** 1-2 months
**Frequency:** 1-2 times weekly (not daily; too harsh)
**For Kids:** Extra gentle; supervise
**Note:** Can be slippery in shower; use caution

## Room Sprays

*Use for: quick aromatherapy, space freshening, mood creation*

### **"Bedroom Sleep Spray"**

**Recipe:**
- 2 cups water
- 1 tablespoon witch hazel or vodka (helps oils disperse)

**Shelf Life:** 1-2 weeks
**Ritual:** Spray bedroom 30 minutes before Murphy's bedtime
**Safety:** Don't spray on face; spray around room or on fabrics

## Sachets & Drawer Fresheners

*Use for: drawer freshening, closet fragrance, lingering scent*

### **"Lavender Sleep Sachet"**

**Recipe:**
- Dried lavender buds (if available) or cotton balls
- 15 drops Lavender essential oil
- 3 drops Frankincense
- Small cloth bag (or coffee filter + string)

**Directions:**
