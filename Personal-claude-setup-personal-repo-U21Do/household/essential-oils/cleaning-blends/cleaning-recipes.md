## All-Purpose Cleaner

*Use for: counters, tables, general surfaces, walls, floors*

### **Recipe:**

**Ingredients:**
- 2 cups water
- 2 tablespoons white vinegar
- 1 tablespoon baking soda
- 10 drops Lemon essential oil
- 5 drops Tea Tree essential oil
- Spray bottle

**Directions:**
1. Pour water into spray bottle
2. Add vinegar and baking soda (will fizz slightly - this is normal!)
3. Wait for fizz to subside
4. Add Lemon and Tea Tree oils
5. Shake well before each use
6. Spray onto surface, wipe with cloth
7. For stubborn grime, let sit 5-10 minutes before wiping

**Shelf Life:** 1-2 weeks
**Safety Notes:**
- Shake well; oils separate from water
- Test on inconspicuous surface first
- Keep away from children during use

**Variations:**
- **Spa-scented:** Swap Lemon + Tea Tree for Lavender + Eucalyptus
- **Citrus-fresh:** Use Lemon + Sweet Orange instead of Tea Tree
- **Garden-inspired:** Use Rosemary + Lemon + Eucalyptus

## Glass & Mirror Cleaner

*Use for: windows, mirrors, glass surfaces, stainless steel*

### **Recipe:**

**Ingredients:**
- 2 cups water
- 2 tablespoons white vinegar
- 10 drops Lemon essential oil
- Spray bottle
- Newspaper or coffee filters (for streak-free finish)

**Directions:**
1. Pour water into spray bottle
2. Add vinegar
3. Add Lemon oil
4. Shake vigorously to combine
5. Spray onto glass
6. Wipe with newspaper or coffee filter (prevents streaks better than paper towels)
7. For tough spots, let sit 2-3 minutes

**Shelf Life:** 1 month
**Pro Tip:** Newspaper works better than paper towels for streak-free shine!

**Variations:**
- **Fresh & bright:** Add 5 drops Sweet Orange for additional freshness
- **Stronger scent:** Add 15 drops Lemon instead of 10

## Bathroom Tile & Grout Cleaner

*Use for: bathroom tiles, grout, shower walls, tile floors*

### **Recipe:**

**Ingredients:**
- 1 cup baking soda
- 1 cup white vinegar
- 10 drops Tea Tree essential oil
- 5 drops Eucalyptus essential oil
- 5 drops Lemon essential oil
- Spray bottle or paste container
- Old toothbrush or small scrub brush

**Directions:**
1. Pour baking soda into bowl
2. Add oils to baking soda
3. Mix with vinegar gradually to create paste (will fizz!)
4. Allow fizz to subside
5. Apply paste to grout/tile with brush
6. Let sit 15-30 minutes for tough buildup
7. Scrub with brush
8. Rinse thoroughly with water

**Shelf Life:** Use immediately; best fresh
**Safety Notes:**
- Ventilate well (vinegar smell is strong)
- Tea Tree + Eucalyptus = powerful antimicrobial combo
- Effective on mold prevention too

**For Mold Prevention:**
- Spray surface with diluted Tea Tree + Eucalyptus + water weekly
- Prevents regrowth and keeps bathroom fresh

## Bathroom Disinfectant Spray

*Use for: toilet, sink, bathtub, mold-prone areas, walls*

### **Recipe:**

**Ingredients:**
- 2 cups water
- 1 tablespoon white vinegar
- 10 drops Tea Tree essential oil
- 5 drops Eucalyptus essential oil
- 2 drops Lemon essential oil
- Spray bottle

**Directions:**
1. Pour water into spray bottle
2. Add vinegar
3. Add all oils
4. Shake vigorously
5. Spray onto surface
6. Let sit 5-10 minutes for disinfection
7. Wipe or scrub as needed
8. Rinse if needed

**Shelf Life:** 1-2 weeks
**Best For:** Mold prevention, antibacterial action, bathroom freshness

**Murphy-Safe Note:** This is non-toxic if accidentally ingested in small amounts (unlike commercial cleaners), but still keep out of reach and supervise use.

## Floor Cleaner

*Use for: hardwood, tile, linoleum, laminate (depending on floor type)*

### **Recipe (For Tile/Linoleum):**

**Ingredients:**
- 2 gallons warm water
- 1 cup white vinegar
- 15 drops Lemon essential oil
- 5 drops Eucalyptus essential oil
- Optional: 1 tablespoon castile soap (for extra cleanliness)
- Mop bucket and mop

**Directions:**
1. Fill bucket with water
2. Add vinegar
3. Add oils
4. Mix well
5. Dip mop, wring out excess
6. Mop floor in sections
7. No rinsing needed (unless very dirty)
8. Allow to air dry

**Shelf Life:** Use within 1-2 weeks
**Note:** For hardwood, use VERY diluted (less vinegar) or use castile soap base instead of vinegar

### **Recipe (For Hardwood - Gentler):**

**Ingredients:**
- 2 gallons warm water
- 1 tablespoon castile soap
- 10 drops Lemon essential oil
- 5 drops Lavender essential oil (optional, for softness)
- Mop bucket and mop

**Directions:**
1. Fill bucket with warm water
2. Add castile soap
3. Add oils
4. Mix gently (avoid excess bubbles)
5. Dip mop, wring thoroughly
6. Mop in sections
7. Air dry
8. Vinegar-free (better for hardwood)

## Kitchen Degreaser

*Use for: stovetop, oven, pots/pans, microwave, appliances*

### **Recipe:**

**Ingredients:**
- 2 cups warm water
- 3 tablespoons castile soap
- 2 tablespoons white vinegar
- 15 drops Lemon essential oil
- 5 drops Orange essential oil (optional)
- Spray bottle or cloth

**Directions:**
1. Mix water and castile soap in spray bottle
2. Add vinegar
3. Add oils
4. Shake well
5. Spray onto greasy surface
6. Let sit 5-10 minutes for tough grease
7. Wipe with cloth or sponge
8. For baked-on grease, make into paste with baking soda

**Shelf Life:** 1-2 weeks
**Pro Tip:** Pair with baking soda paste for oven cleaning

### **For Oven Cleaning (Heavy Grease):**
1. Make paste: 1 cup baking soda + 20 drops Lemon oil + 3 tablespoons water
2. Apply to oven interior (avoid heating elements)
3. Let sit overnight or 12 hours
4. Spray with Glass Cleaner recipe (with vinegar)
5. Wipe clean with cloth
6. Wipe with damp cloth to remove residue

## Laundry Boost

*Add to regular laundry for fresh scent and gentle antibacterial action*

### **Recipe:**

**Ingredients:**
- 10 drops Lemon essential oil
- 5 drops Lavender essential oil
- 5 drops Tea Tree essential oil
- 1 cup white vinegar
- Jar with lid

**Directions:**
1. Pour vinegar into jar
2. Add oils
3. Shake well and let sit 24 hours
4. Shake before each use
5. Add 1/4 cup to rinse cycle (or fabric softener dispenser)
6. Use ½ cup for heavily soiled items

**Shelf Life:** 1 month+
**Safety:** This dilution is safe for all family laundry
**Benefits:** Softens fabric, adds subtle scent, antibacterial action

### **Alternative: Laundry Scent Balls**
- Mix 10 drops Lavender + 5 drops Sweet Orange oil with wool dryer balls
- Toss in dryer with damp load
- Reapply oil every 3-4 uses

## Trash Can & Odor Control

*Use for: trash cans, compost, bathroom odors, pet areas*

### **Recipe:**

**Ingredients:**
- 20 drops Lemon essential oil
- 10 drops Eucalyptus essential oil
- 1 cup baking soda
- Glass jar
- Shaker or cheesecloth

**Directions:**
1. Pour baking soda into jar
2. Add oils drop by drop, stirring with fork
3. Break up clumps as oil absorbs
4. Let sit 24 hours (oil will fully absorb)
5. Shake into trash can, compost, or odorous areas
6. Sprinkle fresh baking soda weekly as needed

**Shelf Life:** 2-3 months
**Use:** Sprinkle in trash daily, compost weekly, bathroom odor control as needed

**For Bathroom Odor Control:**
- Place jar with shaker top under sink
- Shake into toilet area after use
- Very effective and non-toxic

## Air Freshener Spray

*Use for: any room, after cooking, quick freshness*

### **Recipe:**

**Ingredients:**
- 2 cups water
- 1 tablespoon baking soda
- 15 drops Lemon essential oil
- 10 drops Lavender essential oil
- Spray bottle

**Directions:**
1. Pour water into spray bottle
2. Add baking soda (helps disperse oils)
3. Add oils
4. Shake vigorously
5. Spray in air (not on furniture) for immediate freshness
6. Shake well before each use

**Shelf Life:** 1-2 weeks
**Best For:** Quick room freshening, after cooking, before guests

**Variations by Room:**
- **Living Room:** Lemon + Bergamot + Lavender (inviting)
- **Kitchen:** Lemon + Orange + Peppermint (fresh, energizing)
- **Bathroom:** Tea Tree + Eucalyptus + Lemon (clean)
- **Bedroom:** Lavender + Frankincense + Sweet Orange (calming)

## Furniture Polish

*Use for: wood furniture, tables, cabinets*

### **Recipe:**

**Ingredients:**
- 1 cup coconut oil or jojoba oil
- 15 drops Lemon essential oil
- 5 drops Sweet Orange essential oil
- Glass jar
- Soft cloth

**Directions:**
1. Pour oil into jar
2. Add oils
3. Shake well to combine
4. Apply small amount to soft cloth
5. Rub onto wood surface in direction of grain
6. Buff with clean cloth for shine
7. Use sparingly (a little goes a long way)

**Shelf Life:** 2-3 months
**Storage:** Keep in cool, dark place
**Note:** Not suitable for all wood finishes; test first

## Carpet Freshener

*Use for: carpets, rugs, upholstered furniture*

### **Recipe:**

**Ingredients:**
- 2 cups baking soda
- 20 drops Lavender essential oil
- 10 drops Lemon essential oil
- Mason jar with shaker lid
- Fork

**Directions:**
1. Pour baking soda into jar
2. Add oils drop by drop
3. Stir with fork, breaking up clumps as oil absorbs
4. Let sit 24 hours for full absorption
5. Apply: Sprinkle lightly over carpet/furniture
6. Let sit 5-10 minutes
7. Vacuum thoroughly

**Shelf Life:** 3 months+
**Reapply:** Every 1-2 weeks or as needed
**Alternative Scents:** Try Sweet Orange + Frankincense, or Eucalyptus + Lemon

## Dishwasher Detergent (Additive)

*Use as: booster for regular dishwasher detergent*

### **Recipe:**

**Ingredients:**
- 10 drops Lemon essential oil
- 5 drops Tea Tree essential oil
- 1 tablespoon white vinegar
- 1 tablespoon baking soda

**Directions:**
1. Mix baking soda and vinegar in small bowl (will fizz)
2. Add oils to mixture
3. Let fizz subside
4. Add to regular dishwasher detergent scoop (about 1/4 of amount)
5. Use as normal

**Safety:** Ensure all residue is removed before use to avoid damaging dishwasher

## Natural Disinfectant for High-Touch Surfaces

*Use for: doorknobs, light switches, remote controls, phones*

### **Recipe:**

**Ingredients:**
- 1 cup water
- 2 tablespoons isopropyl alcohol (70%)
- 10 drops Tea Tree essential oil
- 5 drops Eucalyptus essential oil
- 5 drops Lemon essential oil
- Small spray bottle

**Directions:**
1. Pour water into bottle
2. Add alcohol
3. Add oils
4. Shake vigorously
5. Spray onto cloth (not directly on electronics)
6. Wipe surface thoroughly
7. Allow to air dry

**Shelf Life:** 1 month+
**Safety Notes:**
- Avoid inhalation of fumes (ventilate area)
- Don't spray directly on electronics
- Keep away from children

**When to Use:**
- During cold/flu season
- When family member is sick
- Weekly high-touch surface maintenance

## Safety & Storage Tips

### **Important Safety Notes:**
✅ **Do:** Always dilute oils properly
✅ **Do:** Test on inconspicuous surface first
✅ **Do:** Label all homemade cleaners clearly
✅ **Do:** Keep away from children and pets during use
✅ **Do:** Ventilate areas well when using vinegar-based cleaners
✅ **Do:** Wear gloves if you have sensitive skin

❌ **Don't:** Mix with bleach or ammonia (creates toxic gas)
❌ **Don't:** Use undiluted oils on surfaces
❌ **Don't:** Leave on surfaces without supervision
❌ **Don't:** Use on delicate fabrics without testing
❌ **Don't:** Store in plastic bottles (oils can degrade plastic)

### **Storage Best Practices:**
- Store in **glass bottles** with tight lids
- Keep in **cool, dark place** (not under sink where heat/moisture accumulates)
- Label clearly with **contents and date**
- Keep separate from food items
- Store away from direct sunlight
- Most recipes last **1-2 weeks to 1 month**
