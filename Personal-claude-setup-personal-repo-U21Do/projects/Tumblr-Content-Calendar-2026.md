# The Becoming Blog: Joy's 2026 Tumblr Content Calendar

**a year of dirt, stars, deconstruction, and unhinged joy**

---

## How This Works

Every day has a **post type** and a **prompt**. Some days are deep. Some days are chaotic. That's the point. You're not performing consistency — you're documenting a life mid-transformation.

**Post types rotate through 7 weekly lanes:**

| Day | Lane | Vibe |
|-----|------|------|
| **Monday** | Dirt & Bloom | Garden life, nature photography, seasonal beauty |
| **Tuesday** | Steel Sharpens Steel | NFL, sports culture, competitive joy |
| **Wednesday** | Smoke & Stone | Spiritual practice, crystals, herbs, tarot, ritual |
| **Thursday** | Raising Humans | Parenting, family chaos, real motherhood |
| **Friday** | Unfiltered | ADHD brain dumps, hot takes, shitposts, memes |
| **Saturday** | Star Maps | Astrology, moon phases, birth chart musings |
| **Sunday** | The Deep End | Deconstruction, therapy thoughts, essays, becoming |

**Photography prompts are woven in** — snap what's real, not what's staged.
