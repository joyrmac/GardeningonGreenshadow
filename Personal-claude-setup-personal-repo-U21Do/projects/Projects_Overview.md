## Steel Sharpens Steel

**File:** `NFL-Content-Guide.txt` (202 lines)

**Full title:** "Steel Sharpens Steel: The Realest Guide to the NFL for Women Who Wanna Know Ball"

### What It Is
Weekly educational NFL content for women learning football — ADHD-friendly formatting, culturally fluent Black fan perspective, no condescension.

### Content Types
| Type | Frequency | Description |
|------|-----------|-------------|
| Weekly Rundown | Tuesdays | Steelers update + 1-2 other teams, league news, player profiles, slang glossary, TL;DR |
| League Literacy Lessons | 1-2x/month | Deep dives on NFL systems (salary cap, free agency, trades, draft) |
| Steelers-Specific Track | Ongoing | Season timeline, draft picks, injury updates, highlights |
| Wildcard Features | As-needed | NFL drama, historical moments, fan culture explainers |

### Focus Teams
Steelers (primary), Eagles, Raiders, 49ers, Saints

### Voice & Tone
Confident, straight-talking, explains jargon in context, never dumbed down. Includes ChatGPT instruction guide for maintaining Joy's voice when generating content.

### Target Audience
Joy herself — and women like her who want to understand football without being talked down to. ADHD-friendly formatting with clear sections, TL;DRs, and visual breaks.
