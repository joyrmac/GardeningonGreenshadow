# ✨ Your Garden Spaces & How to Work Them ✨  
  
  
  
  
  
  
**🌿 Spiral Herb Garden (With Frog Pond)**  
✔️ **Top (Dry, Sunny, Windy)** – Mediterranean herbs: Rosemary, Thyme, Oregano. ✔️ **Southwest (Full Sun)** – Basil, Lavender, Sage. ✔️ **East (Morning Sun, Partial Shade)** – Parsley, Chives. ✔️ **North (Shady, Moist)** – Cilantro, Dill, Lemon Balm (keep mint in a container). 🐸 **Frog Pond Additions** – Pickerel Weed, Water Iris, Creeping Jenny for shelter & oxygenation.  
💡 **Planting Tip**: Use **bricks or stones** to create the spiral height variation. The pond at the bottom will help with **moisture retention** for the lower herbs.  
  
  
**🏡 East-Facing 12’x2’ Bed (Bay Leaf Tree Center)**  
**Challenges:** Limited space, direct morning sun, shade in the afternoon. ✔️ **Drought-tolerant herbs & perennials:** Lavender, Chives, Yarrow, Thyme. ✔️ **Companion for the Bay Tree:** Parsley, Oregano, or Strawflowers. ✔️ **Flowering accents:** Nasturtium, Calendula, or Daisies.  
💡 **Planting Tip**: Since it’s **narrow**, go vertical with a trellis for **Climbing Jasmine or Morning Glories**.  
  
**🌞 10x15 ft Sunny Bed (Screen Room Front)**  
✔️ **East Side (Full Sun)**: Sunflowers, Coneflowers, Zinnias, Cosmos, Milkweed. ✔️ **West Side (Partial Shade)**: Coreopsis, Blueberry Cheesecake Coneflowers, Nasturtiums. ✔️ **North Side (Near House)**: Black Cohosh, Goldenrod, Balloon Milkweed. ✔️ **South Side (Dappled Shade)**: Yarrow, Lobelia, or Foxglove.  
💡 **Planting Tip**: **Stagger bloom times** so something is always in flower!  
  
**🌑 6x4 ft Shaded Bed (Next to Screen Room)**  
✔️ **East Side (Light Sun)**: Coral Bells, Columbine, Hardy Lady Fern. ✔️ **North Side (Deep Shade)**: Hostas, Christmas Ferns, Lily-of-the-Valley. ✔️ **West Side (Near Fence)**: Elephant Ears, Bleeding Hearts. ✔️ **South Side (Partial Shade)**: Foxglove, Astilbe.  
💡 **Planting Tip**: Use **variegated foliage** to brighten shady spots.  
  
**🌾 Backyard Prairie Garden**  
**Purpose:** A **wild, self-sustaining** pollinator paradise! ✔️ **Core Flowers:** Echinacea, Joe Pye Weed, Black-Eyed Susan, Bee Balm. ✔️ **Grasses for Height & Texture:** Little Bluestem, Switchgrass, Eucalyptus Parviflora. ✔️ **Annual Wildflowers for Color:** California Poppies, Cosmos, Zinnias.  
💡 **Planting Tip**: Add **a small walking path** with stone or mulch to admire the space!  
  
**🍂 Pumpkin Patch**  
✔️ **Jack O’Lantern & Small Sugar Pumpkins** – Needs **FULL** sun! ✔️ **Companion plants:** Corn (as a trellis), Nasturtium (pest control), Radishes (soil aeration). ✔️ **Spacing:** Hills 3-4 feet apart, **lots** of room to sprawl.  
💡 **Planting Tip**: Grow **vertically** on a fence for space-saving and air circulation.  
  
**🥕 Vegetable Garden**  
✔️ **Cool-Weather Crops (Spring/Fall):** Carrots, Kale, Lettuce, Broccoli, Cauliflower. ✔️ **Warm-Season Crops (Summer):** Tomatoes, Peppers, Corn, Cucumbers, Melons. ✔️ **Rotation Strategy:** Keep **brassicas separate** from nightshades to prevent soil depletion.  
💡 **Planting Tip**: Use **raised beds** or deep mulch for weed suppression & better drainage.  
  
**🌸 Cut Flower Garden**  
**Best Picks:** Dahlias, Poppies, Sunflowers, Foxglove, Strawflowers, Snapdragons. ✔️ **Fast Bloomers:** Zinnias, Cosmos, Marigolds. ✔️ **Bulbs & Tubers:** Store Dahlias for **next year’s** planting. ✔️ **Pollinator-Friendly Bonus:** Add **Borage & Nasturtium** to bring in bees!  
💡 **Planting Tip**: Cut **often** to encourage **more blooms**!  
  
**🍉 Watermelon & Cantaloupe Patch**  
✔️ **Rows Run North-South for Max Sunlight**. ✔️ **Companions:** Marigolds (pest control), Nasturtiums (trap aphids), Radishes (soil aeration). ✔️ **Spacing:**  
* **Garden Leader Monster Watermelon**: 7 ft apart.  
* **Bush Sugar Baby Watermelon**: 4 ft apart.  
* **Sierra Gold Cantaloupe**: 5 ft apart.  
💡 **Planting Tip**: Use a **trellis** for smaller melons like cantaloupe!  
  
**🌞 Full Sun Landscaping (West-Facing Front Yard)**  
✔️ **Heat-Tolerant & Drought-Resistant Plants:** Coneflowers, Black-Eyed Susans, Nasturtiums, Blue Disc Daisies. ✔️ **Tall Plants for Backdrop:** Sunflowers, Joe Pye Weed, Goldenrod. ✔️ **Low Growing Border Plants:** Alyssum, Creeping Thyme, Sedum.  
💡 **Planting Tip**: Use **mulch heavily** to keep moisture in during the hot afternoons.  
  
**📫 Mailbox & Extra Landscaping Areas**  
✔️ **Bright, Low-Maintenance Flowers:** Zinnias, Nasturtium, Poppies. ✔️ **Fragrant Accents:** Lavender, Rosemary, Citronella. ✔️ **Ground Cover:** Creeping Phlox or Clover.  
💡 **Planting Tip**: Choose plants that **can handle neglect** since this area gets less attention!  
  
**🔥 Next Steps & Priorities**  
1. **🌱 Start Indoors Now** → Peppers, Tomatoes, Herbs, Slow Flowers (Foxglove, Strawflower).  
2. **💨 Cold Stratify** → Milkweed, Eucalyptus, Goldenrod.  
3. **🌞 Plan for Mid-March** → Direct Sow Cool Crops (Carrots, Greens, Poppies).  
4. **🔥 April-May Madness!** → Get **sun-loving flowers & veggies** in the ground.
