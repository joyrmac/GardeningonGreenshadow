# Joy’s Style  
  
For clothing and jewelry that captures the whimsical, practical, and earthy vibe of the character, here are some **brands and styles** that align with her aesthetic:  
  
**Clothing Brands**  
**1. For Practical & Stylish Overalls and Workwear:**  
* **Carhartt:** Rugged, durable overalls and utility-inspired clothing. Look for softer styles in earth tones for a more relaxed vibe.  
* **Dovetail Workwear:** Designed for women, their overalls and work pants combine function and style.  
* **Big Bud Press:** Colorful, retro-inspired overalls and jumpsuits with unique fits and patterns.  
* **Madewell:** Offers stylish, comfortable overalls and jumpsuits in denim or soft fabrics.  
**2. For Cropped Tops and Casual Layers:**  
* **Free People:** Boho-chic cropped tanks and flowy tops that pair well with overalls.  
* **Everlane:** Minimalist, high-quality tanks and tees in neutral and earthy tones.  
* **Patagonia:** Great for lightweight, practical tops that are both eco-conscious and versatile.  
**3. For Flowing Dresses and Whimsical Touches:**  
* **Christy Dawn:** Ethereal, garden-inspired dresses made with sustainable fabrics.  
* **Anthropologie:** Offers feminine yet playful clothing with a hint of sophistication.  
* **Etsy (Vintage Sellers):** Search for vintage or handmade dresses with unique prints or earthy palettes.  
  
**Jewelry Brands**  
**1. For Gold Jewelry:**  
* **Mejuri:** Dainty gold necklaces, earrings, and rings with a clean, modern look.  
* **Catbird NYC:** Whimsical, delicate gold jewelry perfect for layering.  
* **Ana Luisa:** Affordable and sustainable gold jewelry, including simple chains and hoop earrings.  
**2. For Statement Pieces:**  
* **Etsy (Handmade Sellers):** Search for artisan-made gold statement earrings or unique, nature-inspired designs.  
* **Whimsy + Row:** Unique, boho-inspired pieces that have a handmade feel.  
* **SOKO:** Ethical, bold designs made by Kenyan artisans—great for standout earrings or necklaces.  
**3. For Septum Rings and Piercings:**  
* **BVLA:** High-quality, customizable septum rings in gold with beautiful designs.  
* **Body Art Forms:** Wide selection of septum jewelry, from simple gold to intricate designs.  
* **Etsy (Piercing Jewelry):** Search for handmade septum jewelry with unique patterns or gemstone accents.  
  
**Accessories to Complete the Look**  
1. **Wide-Brim Hats:** Look for woven straw or fabric hats from **Brixton** or **Lack of Color** to wear in the garden.  
2. **Leather Belts:** Add a natural leather belt from **Madewell** or **Frye** to accessorize overalls.  
3. **Gardening Gloves:** Cute but functional gloves from **Barebones Living** or **Womanswork**.  
  
**Key Shopping Tips**  
* Stick to earthy tones, natural materials, and sustainable brands for an authentic, grounded vibe.  
* Layer delicate and bold jewelry for a unique, personalized look.  
* Invest in versatile staples like overalls or jumpsuits that can be styled in multiple ways.

Here are **outfit suggestions** tailored to different occasions or settings, inspired by the character’s whimsical, practical, yet stylish vibe. Each includes both clothing and accessories:  
  
**1. Gardening Day: Functional and Cute**  
**Occasion:** Working in the garden, getting hands dirty but staying stylish.  
* **Clothing:**  
    * **Overalls:** **Dovetail Workwear’s Freshley Overall** (earthy green or classic denim).  
    * **Cropped Top:** **Everlane’s Ribbed Tank** in a neutral tone (white, beige, or olive).  
    * **Footwear:** Comfortable waterproof boots like **Blundstone Originals** or **Hunter Chelsea Rain Boots**.  
* **Accessories:**  
    * **Hat:** A wide-brim straw hat from **Brixton** to shield you from the sun.  
    * **Jewelry:** Minimal gold hoops or studs from **Mejuri**, a simple chain necklace.  
    * **Gardening Gloves:** Cute and functional gloves from **Barebones Living**.  
  
**2. Farmer’s Market or Casual Outing**  
**Occasion:** Strolling through a market, visiting friends, or running errands.  
* **Clothing:**  
    * **Top:** A relaxed, cropped linen top from **Free People** in soft pastel or earth tones.  
    * **Bottoms:** High-waisted, wide-leg pants from **Madewell** or a flowy midi skirt from **Anthropologie**.  
    * **Outerwear:** Lightweight denim jacket for layering.  
    * **Footwear:** Leather sandals like **Birkenstock Arizona** or woven flats from **Nisolo**.  
* **Accessories:**  
    * **Jewelry:** Layered gold necklaces from **Catbird NYC** and a statement ring.  
    * **Bag:** A woven basket bag or a canvas tote from **Baggu** for market finds.  
    * **Headband:** A patterned scarf from **Etsy** tied into your curls for a pop of color.  
  
**3. Relaxed Evening in the Garden**  
**Occasion:** Hosting friends or enjoying an evening outdoors.  
* **Clothing:**  
    * **Dress:** A flowy, midi-length dress from **Christy Dawn** in a floral or earthy print.  
    * **Shoes:** Soft leather ankle boots from **Frye** or strappy sandals.  
    * **Layer:** A cozy oversized cardigan for cooler evenings (look for sustainable options from **Everlane**).  
* **Accessories:**  
    * **Jewelry:** Bold gold hoops from **SOKO** or stacked bracelets.  
    * **Hair Accessory:** A claw clip or loose curls styled with a side braid.  
    * **Septum Ring:** A small, elegant gold ring from **Body Art Forms**.  
  
**4. Creative Day or Photoshoot**  
**Occasion:** When you want to channel the character’s whimsical energy, whether for content creation or self-expression.  
* **Clothing:**  
    * **Jumpsuit:** A colorful utility jumpsuit from **Big Bud Press** (go for a rainbow or earthy tone).  
    * **Top:** A fitted crop top underneath for contrast.  
    * **Shoes:** Funky platform sneakers like **Superga Cotu Platforms**.  
* **Accessories:**  
    * **Jewelry:** Layered necklaces, stacked rings, and bold earrings from **Whimsy + Row**.  
    * **Hair:** Let your curls shine with some added texture or mousse (try **DevaCurl SuperCream**).  
    * **Extra Touch:** Add a flower crown or tuck wildflowers into your curls.  
  
**5. Nature Walk or Picnic**  
**Occasion:** Relaxing in nature, hiking, or exploring the outdoors.  
* **Clothing:**  
    * **Top:** A loose, breathable button-up linen shirt from **Eileen Fisher**, tied at the waist.  
    * **Bottoms:** High-rise shorts or breezy culottes from **Patagonia**.  
    * **Footwear:** Durable hiking sandals like **Teva Hurricane** or trail shoes.  
* **Accessories:**  
    * **Hat:** A packable sun hat from **Lack of Color**.  
    * **Jewelry:** Small, minimal gold studs and a leather wrap bracelet.  
    * **Bag:** A lightweight crossbody from **Fjällräven** for essentials.  
  
**General Tips for Matching the Aesthetic:**  
* **Colors:** Stick to earthy, muted, or jewel tones (greens, golds, rusts, deep purples, soft blues).  
* **Materials:** Opt for natural fabrics like linen, cotton, and denim.  
* **Layering:** Mix casual and statement pieces, like functional overalls with bold jewelry.  
* **Jewelry:** Keep it personal—choose pieces that feel meaningful or reflect your personality.  
* **Footwear:** Prioritize comfort without sacrificing style. Boots, sandals, and flats with a rustic flair work best.
