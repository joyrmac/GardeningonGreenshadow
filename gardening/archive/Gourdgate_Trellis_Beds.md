## 🌿 1. Gourdgate Trellis Beds  
**Size**: 2 beds, each 3x6 ft **What to Plant**:  
* Pumpkins: *Jack O’Lantern*, *Small Sugar*  
* Borage  
* Nasturtiums (edge plant for pest control)  
  
## 🌽 2. Corn Sunburst Field  
**Size**: Circular layout, approx. 8–10 ft diameter **What to Plant**:  
* 7 varieties of corn (1 per “ray”)  
* Pole beans at base of each corn stalk  
* Summer Squash & Spaghetti Squash between rays  
  
## 🌱 3. Polyculture Chaos Bed  
**Size**: 4x6 ft or 3x6 ft **What to Plant** (scatter-style):  
* Arugula, Mustard greens, Lettuce blends, Spinach  
* Carrots: Danvers, Parisienne, Purple Dragon, Rainbow, Tendersweet  
* Parsley, Dill, Chives, Fennel  
* Calendula for pollinators  
  
## 🍅 4. Tomato & Pepper Wall Bed  
**Size**: Approx. 3x10 ft with trellis **What to Plant**:  
* Tomatoes: *Early Doll*, *Cherokee Purple*, *Yellow Pear*, *Small Red Cherry*  
* Peppers: *Carranza*, *Nibbler Red*, *Shishito*, *Ancho/Poblano*, *Dragon Roll*, *Jalapeño*  
* Companion herbs/flowers: Basil, Oregano, Marigolds, Calendula  
  
## 🥒 5. Cucumber & Bean Trellis Bed  
**Size**: 3x6 ft with cattle panel **What to Plant**:  
* Cucumber: *Mexican Sour Gherkin*  
* Pole Beans (overflow from corn if needed)  
* Bush Beans: *Landreth Stringless*  
* Companion plants: Nasturtiums, Borage, Dill  
  
## 🍉 6. Melon Moon Bed  
**Size**: 4x8 ft **What to Plant**:  
* Watermelon: *Bush Sugar Baby* (ground grower)  
* Watermelon: *Garden Leader Monster* (trellised)  
* Cantaloupe: *Sierra Gold* (trellised)  
* Companion plants: Marigolds, Hyssop, Mint (in pots)  
  
## 🥦 7. Cool Crop Bed  
**Size**: 4x8 ft **What to Plant**:  
* Broccoli: *Calabrese*  
* Cauliflower: *Snowball Y Improved*  
* Kale: *Garden Blend*  
* Celery: *Tango Hybrid*  
* Spinach: *Bloomsdale*  
* Onion: *Yellow Sweet Spanish*, *White Nebuka Bunching*  
* Companion herbs: Parsley, Thyme  
  
## 🧅 8. Root & Onion Strip  
**Size**: 3x10 ft or a long edge bed **What to Plant**:  
* Carrots (all 5 types again if needed)  
* Onions: Yellow Sweet Spanish & White Nebuka  
* Fennel  
* Companion flowers: Chamomile, Calendula  
* Basil on outer edge for pest protection
