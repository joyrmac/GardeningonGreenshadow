### 2. Cold-Stratify Milkweed — Start Today
You have 7 milkweed varieties. They NEED 30 days in the fridge before they'll germinate.
- [ ] Wet a paper towel, place milkweed seeds on it, fold, put in a ziplock bag
- [ ] Label each bag (variety name + today's date)
- [ ] Put in fridge — they'll be ready to plant around April 8
- [ ] Do the same for Goldenrod (same treatment, 30 days)

### 3. Cold-Stratify Lavender Too
You have 4 lavender varieties (Hidcote Blue, Spike, Vera, Munstead). They need 2-4 weeks cold.
- [ ] Same paper towel method as milkweed
- [ ] These can go in soil indoors in late March/early April
