## Detailed Tasks

### Butterfly Puddler Build (Saturday, March 22 — 1-2 hours)

**Materials Needed:**
- Vintage ceramic bowl or stone dish (12-18" diameter)
- Play sand or fine construction sand (2-3 lbs)
- River pebbles (2-3 lbs)
- Raw crystals: clear quartz, amethyst, citrine (2-4 pieces)
- Mica flakes (small handful)
- Water (collected rainwater preferred)
- Creeping thyme, sedum, or moss (for border)

**Steps:**
1. Choose location near Bloomveil southern edge (dappled shade)
2. Set vessel on level ground
3. Layer bottom: 1" damp sand, 1" pea gravel + pebbles, mica flakes scattered
4. Nestle 2-4 crystals in sand (just poke tips out)
5. Fill with water to just below rim
6. Plant border: 3-5 creeping thyme or sedum around base
7. Water deeply, especially new plants

**Styling:**
- Butterflyweed (2-3) to south
- Coneflowers (2-3) to east
- Lavender cluster to west
- Tall cosmos/joe-pye-weed to north (backdrop)
- Optional: moss-covered stones, feathers, hanging bell on nearby shepherd's crook
