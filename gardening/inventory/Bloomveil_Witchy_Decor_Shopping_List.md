## Sourcing Tips

### Best Deals
- **Thrift stores (Goodwill, Salvation Army, estate sales):** Vintage vessels, brass watering cans, wind chimes. Often $2–$8 each.
- **Michaels/craft stores:** Signs, paint, brushes (with 40% coupon). Check weekly sales.
- **Amazon:** Crystals (compare reviews; stick to reputable sellers with 4.5+ stars). Bulk orders.
- **Etsy:** Unique items, custom painted signs, rare crystals. Budget 1-2 weeks shipping.
- **Local native plant nurseries:** Best source for butterflyweed, native lavender, thyme. Support local!

### Money-Savers
- **Forage freely:** Feathers, branches, moss from your property = $0.
- **DIY paint signs:** Thrift wooden boards or buy blanks cheap. Paint yourself (fun!). Save $15–$30 per sign.
- **Collect stones:** Use rocks from your property or nearby creek for borders instead of buying gravel.
- **Skip new—go vintage:** Thrift stores beat garden center prices on vessels & decor 3:1.
