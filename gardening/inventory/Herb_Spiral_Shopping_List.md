## Notes
- Buy established plants if you want faster establishment (vs seeds)
- Choose 4" or smaller pots for mints/lemon verbena (less transplant shock)
- Plant thyme varieties in spring or early fall
- Chamomile can also be direct-sown from seed
- Winter savory is evergreen—great winter interest!
