## 2. AGRONOMIC CONCERN

**Onion 'Yellow Sweet Spanish, Long Day'** — Long-day onions need 14-16 hours of daylight to bulb. Fuquay-Varina (latitude 35.6N) maxes out around 14.5 hours in June. This variety may produce small bulbs or fail to bulb entirely.

**Recommendation:** Plant it and see what happens, but for reliable onion harvests, order **short-day** (Vidalia, Texas 1015) or **intermediate-day** (Candy, Superstar) varieties for Zone 7b. The Evergreen White Nebuka bunching onion is fine — bunching onions don't depend on day length.
