## Notes

- **Location:** The Gloam (property entrance)
- **Sun:** Full sun (6+ hours)
- **Mulch:** Apply 2-3" around plants, 1" away from crowns
- **Water:** Soil-level watering, 1.5-2" per week
- **Companion planting:** Maximizes pollination, deters pests naturally
- **Harvest:** Begins mid-June, peaks July-September
