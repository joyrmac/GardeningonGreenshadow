# Potion Patch — PRACTICAL POSITIONING GUIDE

**Goal:** Clear, no-confusion planting positions with exact measurements

---

## STEP 1: MARK THE CIRCLE (Before planting anything)

### What you need:
- 10 ft of string or rope
- A stake or nail
- Measuring tape

### How to mark it:
1. **Find center point:** Mark with a stake (this is where you'll stand)
2. **Tie string to stake:** 5 ft long (radius of the circle)
3. **Walk around in circle:** Keep string taut, mark the edge every 2 ft
4. **Connect marks:** You now have a 10 ft diameter circle

```
        String 5 ft long
        tied to center stake
               │
               ↓
        ┌──────●──────┐
        │              │
        │   (CENTER)   │  10 ft total
        │   STAKE      │  diameter
        │              │
        └──────────────┘
         ↑ Mark edge ↑
```

### Mark the C-shape opening:
- **North side:** Plant side (where tall plants go)
- **South side:** OPEN (no plants here - just mulch)
- Mark a **2-ft gap on the south side** = your C-shape opening

---

## STEP 2: DIVIDE THE CIRCLES (Draw on ground with spray paint)

### Center circle (standing area):
- **Diameter:** 18-24" (about 2 ft)
- **Mark:** Spray paint a circle or rope around the 5 ft mark from north

### Ring 1 (Center tall plants):
- **Inner edge:** Center standing circle (2 ft diameter)
- **Outer edge:** 5 ft from center
- **Width:** About 18-24" ring of planting space
- **Where:** North side + East/West sides (NOT south)

### Ring 2 (Middle medium plants):
- **Inner edge:** End of Ring 1 (~5 ft from center)
- **Outer edge:** 8 ft from center
- **Width:** About 18-24" ring
- **Where:** Continues all around except open south

### Ring 3 (Outer short plants):
- **Inner edge:** End of Ring 2 (~8 ft from center)
- **Outer edge:** 10 ft from center (the boundary)
- **Width:** About 18-24" ring (your edge)
- **Where:** All around except the 2 ft gap on south

1. From center stake, measure 5 ft north = plant Valerian there (2 plants, 24" apart E-W)
2. Around that area (4-5 ft from center) scatter Echinacea, Mullein, Angelica
3. Leave 18-24" between each plant type
4. All roughly north-facing side

**Height layering creates a natural amphitheater effect:**
- Ring 1: 3-6 ft (back layer, shade-free)
- Ring 2: 1.5-3 ft (mid-layer)
- Ring 3: 1-6 ft (front layer, with Hollyhock creating dramatic backdrop)

## STEP 6: QUICK REFERENCE - PLANTING ORDER

**Plant in this order (easier management):**

### Week 1 (Late March)
1. **Mark circle** (string + stakes)
2. **Plant Ring 1 center perennials** (Valerian, Echinacea, Angelica)
   - All north side
   - Space carefully

### Week 2 (Early April)
3. **Plant Ring 2 perennials** (Astragalus, St. John's Wort, Yarrow, Hyssop, Horehound)
   - Distributed around north/east/west
   - Avoid south opening

### Week 3 (Mid-April)
4. **Plant Ring 1 remaining** (Mullein if starting from seed/seedling)

### Week 4 (Late May / Early June - FROST SAFE DATE)
5. **Direct sow Ring 3 annuals** (Calendula, Chamomile, Borage, Fenugreek, Marigold, Anise, Hollyhock)
   - Scatter seeds where marked
   - Water daily for 2 weeks
   - Thin at 2-4 weeks
