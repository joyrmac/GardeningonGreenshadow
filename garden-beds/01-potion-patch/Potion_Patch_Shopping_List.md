## MONEY-SAVING TIPS

1. **Start from seed where possible** (Yarrow, Hyssop, Mullein, Calendula, Chamomile, Borage)
   - **Save:** $3-5 per plant

2. **Bulk seed packets** (not individual)
   - **Save:** 50% vs. single-use packets

3. **Compost from home** (if you have it)
   - **Save:** $15-30

4. **Ask gardening friends** for divisions/seedlings
   - **Save:** $30-50

5. **Wait for late-season nursery sales** (July-August)
   - **Save:** 20-30% if planting biennials in fall

6. **Mullein from wild** (find volunteers in area, transplant)
   - **Save:** $2-4

## PLANTING TIMELINE & ORDERING

### March
- **Order:** Perennial starters (1-2 weeks before planting)
- **Cost:** ~$50
- **Action:** Pick up early April, plant immediately

### April
- **Order:** Biennial starters (Hollyhock)
- **Order:** Early-germinating seeds (Yarrow, Hyssop, Mullein if starting from seed)
- **Cost:** ~$20
- **Action:** Direct sow or plant starters

### May
- **Order:** Annual seeds for direct sowing
- **Cost:** ~$15
- **Action:** Prepare soil, direct sow end of May / early June

### June onwards
- **Maintain:** Deadhead, thin seedlings, water
- **Cost:** $0
