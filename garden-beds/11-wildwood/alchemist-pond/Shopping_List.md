## Water Plants

| Plant | Where to Buy | Est. Cost | Notes |
|-------|-------------|-----------|-------|
| **Blue Flag Iris** | [Pond Megastore](https://pondmegastore.com/products/blue-flag-iris) or [The Pond Guy](https://www.thepondguy.com/product/blue-flag-iris/) | $8-15 | Native. Get 2-3 for good coverage. |
| **Dwarf/Miniature Cattail** | [Pond Megastore](https://pondmegastore.com/collections/shelf-plants-bog-plants-shallow-water-aquatic-plants) | $8-12 | Make sure it says "miniature" or "dwarf" — regular cattails will take over. |
| **Water Lettuce** | [Happy Frog Aquatics](https://www.happyfrogaquatics.com/collections/floating-pond-plants) | $8-15 | Floating. Will multiply on its own. |
| **Duckweed** | [Aquatic Arts](https://aquaticarts.com/products/duckweed) or [Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/LIVE-DUCKWEED-PLANTS-LEMNA-MINOR/dp/B00F83F8ZG) | $8-12 | A little goes a LONG way. It multiplies fast. |
| **Water Mint** | Pond Megastore or local nursery | $6-10 | Check if Logan's carries it. Plant in a pot — it spreads. |
| **Mesh aquatic plant pots** (assorted sizes) | Amazon or pond suppliers | $10-15 | For planting iris, cattail, mint in. Prevents spreading. |
| **Aquatic planting soil** (1 small bag) | Amazon or pond suppliers | $8-12 | Heavy clay-based. NOT regular potting mix (it floats). |
| **Creeping Jenny** | **YOU HAVE THIS — free cuttings from your porch planter** | $0 | Trail cuttings over the rim rocks. |

## Where to Check Locally First

Before ordering online, check these places in the Fuquay area:
- **Logan's Garden Hut** — they may carry water plants, pond supplies, and garden decor
- **Home Depot / Lowe's** — pond liner supplies, gravel, PVC pipe, basic pond plants in season
- **Local pond/aquatic shops** — better selection of healthy water plants than big box stores
- **Thrift stores** — cauldrons, gazing balls, mirrors, garden decor for cheap
- **Your own yard** — creeping jenny, flat rocks, logs, moss, ferns
