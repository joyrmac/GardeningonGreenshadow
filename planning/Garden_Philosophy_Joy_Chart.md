## What This Looks Like in Wildwood: Specific Design Principles

### **Water**
- Minimum: Bird bath or small fountain in a quiet zone
- Medium: Rain garden or bog garden bed that celebrates water
- Ideal: A small pond or stream feature, even 3x6 ft, creates the Pisces anchor

### **Solitude Zones**
- At least one hidden sitting spot (bench surrounded by screening plants)
- A "maker's corner" (small table/chair for sketching, journaling, planning)
- Plant trees/shrubs to create rooms, not open space

### **Color Palette**
Lean into jewel tones and drama — **NOT** the typical pastels:
- Deep purples (salvias, delphiniums)
- Rich reds (dahlias, cannas, crocosmia)
- Jewel-tone blues (agapanthus, morning glories)
- Burnt orange (calendula, nasturtium, daylilies)
- Deep greens (ferns, hostas, evergreens as anchors)
- Gold accents (rudbeckia, sunflowers, golden foliage)

### **Productive Beds with Drama**
Melons, cucumbers, beans don't have to be utilitarian:
- Train melons on sculptural trellises (not invisible)
- Let cucumbers climb in dramatic walls of green
- Plant beans with ornamental flowers (borage = edible + beautiful)
- Harvest vegetables as a *celebration*, not a chore
- Vegetable beds are part of the garden's visual story

### **Paths & Navigation**
- Mulched or stone paths (clear, defined, organized)
- But allow plants to billow over the edges (soft, flowing)
- Meander rather than straight lines (Pisces-friendly)
- Somewhere to pause and sit (benches at path intersections)

### **Companion Planting as Aesthetic**
Don't hide companions; celebrate them:
- Borage (edible flowers, beautiful blue) alongside vegetables
- Nasturtium (trap crop + gorgeous) as visual interest
- Herbs that flower (salvias, oregano, catmint) as ornamental
- Beneficial insects get shelter AND the garden gets beauty

### **Full Moon Tension in Action**
Example from a 10x15 bed (like your new melon/cucumber bed):
- **Back half:** Melons sprawling, wild-ish, following their nature (Pisces + Mars energy)
- **Front half:** Cucumber trellis, architectural, clearly structured (Virgo energy)
- **Result:** One bed that holds both your nature
