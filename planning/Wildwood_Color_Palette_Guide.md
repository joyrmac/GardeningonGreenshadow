# Wildwood Color Palette Guide

*Your complete reference for every bed's color story*

---

## The Philosophy

You have a Virgo brain that wants everything organized AND a Leo stellium that wants DRAMA. This color palette honors both.

- **Virgo:** Each bed has a defined color scheme (organized, not chaotic)
- **Leo:** Every palette uses jewel tones and boldness (not pastels, not timid)
- **Pisces:** Water blues and cool tones woven throughout (calming, emotional)
- **Full Moon:** Structures hold color, but plants are allowed to spill (both, held in tension)

## HOW TO USE THIS GUIDE

1. **When planting:** Check the color for your bed. Stick to the palette. This isn't restrictive — it's organizational.

2. **When shopping for seeds/plants:** Look for the colors in YOUR bed's section. Ask yourself: "Does this fit the story?"

3. **When you're doubting a choice:** Ask: Does this honor my Leo? Does it honor my Virgo? Both?

4. **When succession planting:** Same color family = visual continuity. Rotate timing = continuous blooms.

5. **When harvesting:** Celebrate the colors you've grown. Take photos. Notice how the bed changes through the season.
