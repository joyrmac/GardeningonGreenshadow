# 🪴 PORCH PLANTER PLANS (3 BIG POTS)  
  
🌞** POT 1 — The Sunny Sentinel**  
  
**Sun, heat, toughness. This pot works for a living.**  
  
**Plants**  
	•	**Purple Coneflower (Echinacea)** – center anchor 🌸  
	•	*Where:* Home Depot (Perennials section)  
	•	**Black-Eyed Susan (Rudbeckia)** – companion bloom 🌼  
	•	*Where:* Home Depot

🏬** HOME DEPOT — SOIL REFRESH (VERY IMPORTANT)**  
  
Your pots are “full,” yes—but the soil is **tired**, like a woman who raised kids and deserves better. 😤  
  
**Buy:**  
	•	**1 bag compost** (Black Kow or similar) 🐄  
	•	**1 small bag worm castings** (optional but excellent) 🪱  
	•	**1 small bag pine bark fines OR aged bark mulch** 🌲  
  
**How to amend (do this before planting):**  
	1.	Scoop out top **4–6 inches** of old soil  
	2.	Mix:  
	•	70% existing soil  
	•	20–25% compost  
	•	5–10% worm castings (if using)  
	3.	Refill pots, leaving 1–2 inches at top  
	4.	Top with **bark mulch** (not decorative rocks)  
  
🧓🏼 *This feeds roots slowly. No chemical nonsense.*
